## Overview

The Noontec Hammo GO is a wired, over-ear active noise cancelling headphone featuring SCCB (Surround Closed Cavity Body) and Votrik HD500 50mm drivers [3]. A third-party review lists a driver frequency spectrum of 5Hz–30,000Hz (as published in the review source) and describes ANC using AAA batteries via an on-cup switch [1]. Noontec lists the Hammo GO under its noise cancelling lineup with a current price of 119.95 USD and describes dual operation: music playback works with or without battery, while noise cancelling requires power [2][3]. The product emphasizes foldable portability, a hard carry case, and protein-cotton ear cushions [1][3].
