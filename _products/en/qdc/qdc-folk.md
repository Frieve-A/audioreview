## Overview

QDC Folk represents a premium in-ear monitor developed by Chinese manufacturer Shenzhen Qili Audio Application Co., Ltd. (QDC) [1]. Building on over two decades of military communication experience, the company created this IEM with emphasis on handcrafted aesthetics and premium materials [3]. Developed in collaboration with Chinese folk band "Xiaojuan and the residents in the Valley," the Folk targets acoustic and vocal music enthusiasts with handcrafted Phoebe wood face covers, swappable plug connectivity (3.5mm/2.5mm/4.4mm), and premium packaging [1]. At 428 USD, it positions itself in the mid-to-high price segment with emphasis on aesthetic craftsmanship, musical collaboration, and multi-plug functionality rather than pure technical performance [1].
