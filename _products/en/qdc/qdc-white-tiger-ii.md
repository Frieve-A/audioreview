## Overview

QDC White Tiger II is a premium hybrid in-ear monitor featuring 6 balanced armature drivers and 2 electrostatic drivers retailing at 1,359.15 USD for the standard version[5]. Developed through collaboration between QDC and Aiuto, this model implements the company's proprietary Multitube Crossover Technology, ships with a resin shell, 0.78 mm 2-pin interface, and a bundled 3-in-1 cable that swaps between 3.5 mm, 2.5 mm, and 4.4 mm plugs[5]. The White Tiger II covers 10 Hz–70 kHz, offers 105 dB SPL/mW sensitivity, 15-ohm impedance, and 26 dB of passive isolation, targeting measurement-focused users seeking sophisticated driver integration[5].
