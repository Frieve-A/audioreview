## Overview

The INAWAKEN DAWN Ms is a high-end in-ear monitor featuring an 11.2mm dynamic driver with proprietary purple-gold sputter deposition diaphragm technology. Priced at 4,999 USD, this IEM represents INAWAKEN's flagship entry into the ultra-premium market segment. The company claims exceptional technical specifications including THD below 0.05% and frequency response variation under 0.5dB, positioning this product as a measurement-focused offering in the high-end category. The DAWN Ms incorporates advanced manufacturing techniques including precision CNC machining, medical-grade transparent resin shells, and a proprietary "Bass Superposition System" developed in collaboration with 3D printing specialist Heygears over 13 months of refinement.
