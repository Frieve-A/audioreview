## Overview

The REQST R-Pad-GP18 is an audio vibration accessory measuring 68mm × 58mm × 6mm and weighing 8.5 grams. Constructed from genuine leather in wine red color with embedded vibration-damping ceramic, the product claims to improve sound quality when placed on or under audio equipment. REQST markets this as applying "dynamic vibration absorber" technology used for wind-sway countermeasures in high-rise buildings to audio applications. The product features a 6.5mm hole for cable management and sells for 76 USD. This represents REQST's third-generation approach to vibration control accessories, targeting audiophiles seeking passive tweaks for their systems.
