## Overview

The Questyle QCC Dongle Pro Lossless Bluetooth Transmitter represents the company's entry into the wireless audio transmission market. Built around Qualcomm's QCC3086 chip with Bluetooth 5.4 technology, this USB-C powered device supports a comprehensive range of audio codecs including aptX Lossless and LDAC up to 990kbps. The device distinguishes itself as the world's first lossless Bluetooth transmitter certified under Apple's MFi program, targeting users seeking high-quality wireless audio transmission from various source devices including gaming consoles and computers.

## Scientific Validity

As a digital Bluetooth transmitter priced at 99 USD, the QCC Dongle Pro provides legitimate functional improvements through comprehensive codec support including LDAC and aptX Lossless. The device specifications include Bluetooth 5.4, transmission range of ≥10m, ultra-low latency as low as 48ms, and power consumption of 37mA during LDAC operation at 48kHz/24bit. However, no actual audio measurement results such as THD, SNR, frequency response, jitter, or digital conversion accuracy are publicly available from third-party sources. While the device claims low latency and efficient power consumption, these specifications lack independent verification.
