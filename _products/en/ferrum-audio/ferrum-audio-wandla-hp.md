## Overview

The Ferrum Audio WANDLA HP is a DAC/preamp with integrated headphone amplifier priced at 3,295 USD [1]. Built around the ESS Sabre ES9038PRO DAC chip, it features a fully balanced composite IC headphone amplifier capable of 3.5 watts output at 50 ohms balanced. The unit is positioned between entry-level and flagship headphone audio equipment, targeting enthusiasts seeking more than basic performance. Key features include comprehensive digital connectivity with six inputs (USB-C, AES/EBU, optical, coaxial, ARC, I2S), support for 768 kHz/32-bit PCM and DSD512, MQA decoder/renderer capability, and compatibility with Ferrum's HYPSOS power supply system. The device represents Ferrum's engineering approach of custom circuit design rather than off-the-shelf solutions.

## Scientific Validity

$$ \Large \text{0.8} $$

Manufacturer specifications indicate excellent performance across all key indicators. Official specifications show THD of 0.00016% balanced and 0.00022% unbalanced [1], well below the 0.01% transparent threshold. Dynamic range measures 122dB [1], substantially exceeding the 105dB transparent level. Output impedance of 0.5 ohms unbalanced and 1 ohm balanced [1] provides excellent headphone compatibility. Output power of 3.5 watts at 50 ohms balanced and 0.75 watts at 300 ohms balanced [1] offers sufficient drive capability for intended applications. SoundStage! Hi-Fi reports manufacturer-provided specifications of 0.00009% THD, 127dB dynamic range, and -115dB THD+N [2]. While independent third-party measurements are referenced as available, the evaluation relies primarily on manufacturer specifications pending independent verification. Conservative scoring applied due to limited verified third-party measurement data, though all manufacturer specifications indicate performance well within excellent ranges for the headphone amplifier category.

## Technology Level

$$ \Large \text{0.9} $$

Advanced proprietary technology implementation with significant innovation. Ferrum developed a custom current-to-voltage converter specifically tailored for the ES9038PRO DAC chip [3], representing in-house design expertise rather than off-the-shelf solutions. The SERCE daughterboard provides ARM-based digital processing that eliminates traditional XMOS chips, making five components redundant [3]. This ARM processing platform handles audio stream marshalling and pre-processing across six digital inputs. Custom discrete operational amplifier approach contrasts with ready-made IC solutions used by competitors. Digital filter selection specially prepared by renowned HQ Player demonstrates advanced software integration [3]. The proprietary technology offers competitive advantages requiring years for new entrants to replicate. ARM-based processing combined with software-based filtering represents cutting-edge technology adoption aligned with modern audio engineering trends. Integration of measurement-based engineering methodology with HYPSOS power system using 4TSD voltage-sensing [4] demonstrates sophisticated technical approach.

## Cost-Performance

$$ \Large \text{1.0} $$

No cheaper product exists with equivalent-or-better functionality and measured performance. The WANDLA HP's comprehensive feature set includes six digital inputs (USB-C, AES/EBU, optical, coaxial, ARC, I2S), integrated headphone amplifier, support for 768kHz/32bit PCM and DSD512, MQA decoder/renderer capability, 2.8W balanced output power at 32 ohms, 3.5W balanced output power at 50 ohms, THD 0.00016% (balanced), and dynamic range 122dB [1]. Alternative products like the Topping DX3 Pro+ at 199 USD [5] are inferior due to multiple functional gaps: lacks AES/EBU and I2S professional interfaces essential for studio connectivity, provides only 1.8W versus 2.8W balanced output power at 32 ohms, and omits MQA decoder/renderer functionality required for high-resolution streaming services. The FiiO K5 Pro at 150 USD [5] similarly lacks professional AES/EBU and I2S inputs and provides no MQA support. Professional interfaces including AES/EBU and I2S connectivity are essential for equivalent functionality but unavailable in products under 3,000 USD price range. The WANDLA HP represents the most affordable product combining professional-grade connectivity, high output power, and complete MQA support in a single unit.
