## Overview

The Buchardt Audio A500 was a premium active wireless speaker system that targeted high-end home audio and professional studio monitoring applications. **Note: This product has been discontinued and is no longer available from Buchardt Audio, having been replaced by the Anniversary 10 (A10) model in 2023.** These compact bookshelf speakers feature a sophisticated 2.5-way or 3-way sealed enclosure design with tri-amplified Class D power delivery totaling 450W per speaker. Key innovations include proprietary CDC (Constant Directivity Control) aluminum waveguide technology, WiSA wireless transmission supporting 24-bit/96kHz lossless audio, and comprehensive DSP processing with quad-core processors and multiple CS4398 DAC chips per speaker. The system enables downloadable "master tuning" profiles for customizable sound characteristics and provides extensive connectivity including balanced/unbalanced XLR inputs with 192kHz analog-to-digital conversion capability.

## Scientific Validity

$$ \Large \text{0.8} $$

Comprehensive third-party measurements from Erin's Audio Corner demonstrate exceptional performance across multiple criteria [1]. Frequency response of 25-40kHz (±1.5dB) significantly exceeds transparent level standards, with the ±1.5dB tolerance approaching excellent standard while bass extension to 25Hz surpasses typical bookshelf speaker capabilities. Harmonic distortion measurements at 86dB and 96dB SPL show good performance with acceptable levels at normal listening volumes and controlled behavior at higher outputs [1]. Dynamic range compression testing across multiple reference levels (76dB, 86dB, 96dB, and 102dB) confirms robust power handling without significant compression artifacts [1]. Complete SPINORAMA measurements including horizontal/vertical response, listening window, and sound power demonstrate well-controlled directivity characteristics [1]. Step response and group delay measurements indicate proper time alignment optimization through DSP processing [1]. The comprehensive measurement data confirms performance approaching transparent level across most audible criteria.

## Technology Level

$$ \Large \text{1.0} $$

The A500 demonstrates exceptional technological sophistication through proprietary CDC aluminum waveguide design, quad-core DSP processing with 5,402-point measurement optimization, and advanced WiSA wireless implementation supporting lossless digital transmission. Engineering excellence evidenced by tri-amplified configuration with dedicated CS4398 DAC chips per driver, comprehensive crossover flexibility between 2.5-way and 3-way configurations, and ISO 226:2003 dynamic equalization implementation [2]. The holographic near-field scanner measurement system used in development represents cutting-edge acoustic engineering rarely employed at this price level. Technology integration encompasses advanced wireless protocols, sophisticated room correction algorithms, and downloadable firmware tunings that demonstrate forward-thinking digital audio implementation. Technical achievements position this product at industry-leading level for active speaker technology.

## Cost-Performance

$$ \Large \text{1.0} $$

The A500 was priced at 4,450 USD when available. Extensive research for active wireless speaker systems with equivalent-or-better functionality and measured performance including 25Hz bass extension, 450W output (per speaker), WiSA wireless transmission (24-bit/96kHz), comprehensive DSP processing, 25-40kHz (±1.5dB) frequency response, and third-party measurement data from Erin's Audio Corner revealed no cheaper alternatives. The A500 was the world's cheapest active wireless speaker system with equivalent-or-better functionality and measured performance when available.

## Advice

**The Buchardt Audio A500 is discontinued and no longer available for purchase.** For current buyers seeking similar performance characteristics, consider Buchardt's Anniversary 10 (A10) active speakers which continue the company's wireless DSP approach with updated technology [9]. The A500 represented exceptional measurement-verified performance with 25Hz bass extension and advanced wireless implementation when available. Users previously interested in the A500's capabilities should evaluate the Anniversary 10 model or other current active wireless speakers offering comparable bass extension and DSP functionality. For those finding used A500 units on the secondary market, verify warranty transferability and component availability before purchase. The A500's design philosophy of measurement-based optimization and direct-to-consumer approach influenced subsequent Buchardt products and remains relevant for understanding the brand's technical direction.
