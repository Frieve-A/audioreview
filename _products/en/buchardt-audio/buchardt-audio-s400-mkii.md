## Overview

The Buchardt Audio S400 MKII represents the Danish company's flagship bookshelf speaker, incorporating their proprietary Constant Directivity Control (CDC) aluminum waveguide technology. This 2-way passive radiator system features a custom 0.74" soft fabric textile tweeter paired with a 6" long-stroke paper cone midwoofer and a large 5x8" passive radiator replacing traditional bass ports. At 2,415 USD per pair, the S400 MKII positions itself in the premium bookshelf speaker segment, emphasizing controlled directivity for consistent performance across various listening positions and room environments. The company's direct-sales approach bypasses traditional dealer networks, with manufacturing handled in Denmark using custom drivers and high-end crossover components.

## Scientific Validity

$$ \Large \text{0.6} $$

The S400 MKII demonstrates solid measured performance approaching transparent levels. Third-party measurements from Erin's Audio Corner show excellent on-axis response within 2dB deviation [1], significantly exceeding the ±3.0dB problematic threshold and approaching the ±1.0dB excellent range for speakers. Manufacturer specifications indicate frequency response of 33-40,000Hz (±3dB in-room) with reliable bass extension to 50Hz in third-party verification [1]. The measured sensitivity of 87dB @ 2.83V/1m meets manufacturer claims and represents good amplifier efficiency. Harmonic distortion measurements from Erin's Audio Corner reveal THD performance that varies with output level: at 86dB SPL, distortion remains below 0.5% across most of the frequency range, staying below the 1% problematic threshold for speakers [1]. However, at 96dB SPL, THD rises to 2-3% in the bass region (around 50-80Hz), exceeding the 1% problematic level threshold [1]. Above 200Hz, distortion remains acceptable even at higher output levels. A slight high-frequency boost above 8kHz is noted but remains within acceptable deviation ranges. Critical measurements including signal-to-noise ratio, crosstalk, dynamic range require further independent verification. The available measurement data places this product at the standard-to-good performance level, with controlled directivity performance exceeding typical bookshelf speaker radiation patterns, though high-SPL bass distortion represents a limitation for dynamic material.

## Technology Level

$$ \Large \text{0.8} $$

The S400 MKII demonstrates exceptional technical sophistication through multiple proprietary technologies. The CDC aluminum waveguide represents genuine patent technology developed in-house, providing controlled directivity that competitors actively seek to replicate. Custom driver design by Ulrik Schmidt (creator of the renowned ScanSpeak Revelator series) demonstrates significant technical expertise and industry recognition. The crossover utilizes premium components including Miflex CU/paper/oil capacitors, Jantzen Audio Denmark Cross-caps, baked air-core inductors with high-purity copper wire, and Superes resistors [2]. The passive radiator implementation replaces conventional bass ports, achieving 30% cabinet size reduction while eliminating port noise and compression. The comprehensive development process included over 2,700 driver measurements totaling 5,402 measurement points for systematic optimization [3]. This technology portfolio requires substantial R&D investment and creates competitive advantages lasting 3+ years for market entrants attempting replication.

At 2,415 USD current market price, comprehensive search for equivalent-or-better alternatives with measured performance data reveals no cheaper products meeting the S400 MKII's combination of 33Hz bass extension (±3dB in-room), 87dB sensitivity, excellent on-axis response within 2dB deviation, and controlled directivity performance. Therefore, the S400 MKII represents the world's cheapest option with equivalent-or-better functions and measured performance, resulting in CP = 1.0.

## Reliability & Support

$$ \Large \text{0.8} $$

Buchardt Audio provides exceptional support infrastructure through their direct-sales model. The 10-year warranty significantly exceeds the industry-standard 2-year coverage, locked to the first buyer for comprehensive long-term protection [5]. The global direct manufacturer support system eliminates dealer dependencies, with the company maintaining direct customer communication channels. Unique self-repair design philosophy enables customers to replace components themselves with guided manufacturer support, reducing shipping inconvenience and repair downtime. Post-warranty support includes 50% discounts on replacement parts, ensuring long-term serviceability [6]. The 45-day return period provides adequate evaluation time. However, multiple user reports indicate speakers can move or fall from stands due to bass output vibrations, representing an inherent stability design problem that affects reliable operation [7]. Despite this construction concern, the comprehensive warranty coverage, direct support access, and customer-serviceable design philosophy create strong reliability infrastructure.
