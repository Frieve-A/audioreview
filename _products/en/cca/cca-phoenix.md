## Overview

The CCA Phoenix is a single 10mm dual-magnet dynamic driver in-ear monitor featuring a Liquid Crystal Polymer (LCP) diaphragm and aerospace-grade metal construction. Released in September 2025 as part of CCA's independent product line under the ND Audio umbrella, the Phoenix represents the company's post-KZ evolution toward more refined tuning approaches. The product targets the sub-35 USD market segment with claimed Harman target curve compliance and professional-grade construction including precision 5-axis CNC milling and detachable 0.78mm 2-pin connectors. CCA positions this model as delivering "HiFi Clear Sound & Deep Bass" for musicians, singers, and audiophiles seeking cost-effective audio solutions.

## Cost-Performance

$$ \Large \text{1.0} $$

This evaluation is based solely on functions and numerical performance data. Driver types and configurations (dynamic, planar magnetic, BA, hybrid, horn, etc.) are completely excluded from consideration.

**Product Analysis:**
The CCA Phoenix (29.00 USD) provides 0.78mm 2-pin detachable connectors, multiple connector options (3.5mm, USB-C), frequency response of 20-40,000 Hz, sensitivity of 108 dB, and impedance of 33 Ω.

Features 0.78mm 2-pin detachable connectors, THD ≤0.5%@1kHz, sensitivity 119 dB/Vrms, frequency range 15Hz-38kHz. While Phoenix includes both 3.5mm and USB-C as standard, Chu II DSP is USB-C only.

## Reliability & Support

$$ \Large \text{0.4} $$

The CCA Phoenix receives below-average reliability and support evaluation due to limited warranty coverage and support infrastructure. The product offers only 1-year warranty on the main unit with 3-month cable warranty, falling below industry standard 2-year coverage. Support is provided primarily through distributor channels via Linsoul Audio rather than direct manufacturer infrastructure. The design demonstrates inherent robustness with simple structure featuring aerospace-grade metal construction and detachable cables for serviceability. However, repair services are limited to cable replacement through standard 0.78mm 2-pin connectors, with no documented manufacturer repair centers or comprehensive service network. Support availability is restricted to business hours (09:00-18:00 UTC+8) through distributor channels.

## Advice

For users seeking budget in-ear monitors with solid construction and balanced tuning claims, the CCA Phoenix offers competent engineering with modern materials and detachable cable design. The 29.00 USD price point positions it appropriately within the competitive budget IEM market, though alternatives like the Moondrop Chu II DSP provide equivalent functionality with USB-C connectivity at lower cost with measurement validation. The Phoenix suits users who value metal construction and multiple connector options, particularly those seeking CCA's refined post-KZ design approach. However, the lack of third-party measurement validation and below-standard warranty coverage may concern users prioritizing verified performance and long-term support. Consider measurement-validated alternatives in similar price ranges for greater confidence in audio performance claims.
