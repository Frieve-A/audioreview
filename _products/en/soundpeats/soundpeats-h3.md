## Overview

The SoundPEATS H3 are true wireless earbuds featuring a triple-driver system combining one 12mm dynamic driver with two balanced armature drivers [1]. Powered by Qualcomm QCC3091 with Snapdragon Sound, they support LDAC and aptX Lossless codecs for hi-res audio playback [1]. The earbuds offer AI-powered hybrid ANC with up to 55dB noise reduction, 7-hour battery life with 37 total hours including the case, and comprehensive connectivity features including dual-device pairing [1]. At 129.99 USD, they target the premium budget segment with advanced driver configuration and codec support typically found in higher-priced products.
