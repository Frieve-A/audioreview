## Overview

The TANCHJIM 4U is a professional Hi-Fi in-ear monitor featuring the company's proprietary DMT4 Ultra single dynamic driver technology. Launched after CanJam Singapore 2024, this device combines a full metal brushed finish chassis with innovative 4-stage precision filter system offering four distinct sound profiles: atmosphere, pop, natural, and monitoring modes. The name "4U" represents "For You", emphasizing customizable audio experiences that allow users to adjust output to their preferences. With double-chamber configuration, PU suspended edge LCP dome composite diaphragm, and U-shaped design optimized for comfort, the 4U is positioned as an accessible entry-level to mid-range IEM targeting budget-conscious audio enthusiasts at approximately 70 USD MSRP.

## Technology Level

$$ \Large \text{0.8} $$

The TANCHJIM 4U demonstrates strong proprietary technology implementation through DMT4 Ultra dynamic driver representing the 4th generation of TANCHJIM's driver development. In-house design capabilities are evidenced by proprietary dual-chamber configuration with double-magnetic circuit and LCP composite diaphragm designed using FEA simulation technology. The unique 4-level adjustable circuit filter system provides mechanical bass tuning functionality not commonly found among competitors. While DMT4 represents advanced proprietary driver technology with significant know-how accumulation, it is superseded by the company's newer DMT5 generation, indicating continued innovation trajectory. Technology integration combines appropriate digital simulation with mechanical implementation, though lacks cutting-edge computer or AI integration typical of highest scoring products.

## Reliability & Support

$$ \Large \text{0.5} $$

Warranty period of 1 year on main unit falls below typical 2-year industry standard, with cable warranty varying between 3-6 months depending on retailer. Global manufacturer direct support is available through authorized international retailers. The company provides coverage for manufacturing defects and malfunction due to non-human factors within warranty period. Moderate design complexity with dual-chamber driver and 4-level adjustable filter system presents average reliability expectations. Known minor issues include fingerprint-prone glossy metal faceplates, chin slider position retention problems, and cable tangling tendency, though no critical defects or widespread failure reports are documented. Construction utilizes zinc alloy with mirror finish and LCP composite materials suggesting adequate durability for portable use.

## Advice

The TANCHJIM 4U targets users seeking customizable sound signatures in a single product without requiring multiple purchases. The 4-level adjustable filter system provides atmosphere, pop, natural, and monitoring modes for varied listening preferences. At 69.99 USD MSRP, the product offers proprietary DMT4 Ultra technology with claimed excellent THD performance, though lack of independent measurement verification requires consideration.
