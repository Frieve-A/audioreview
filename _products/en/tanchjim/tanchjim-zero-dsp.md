## Overview

The TANCHJIM ZERO DSP is an in-ear monitor featuring USB-C connectivity with integrated dual 24-bit DACs and digital signal processing capabilities. Built around TANCHJIM's proprietary DMT4 dynamic driver technology, the product incorporates a 10mm composite diaphragm with beryllium-plated dome and flexible microfiber suspension. The earphone supports 44.1kHz, 48kHz, and 96kHz sampling rates through a Type-C cable with silver-plated oxygen-free copper construction. Founded in 2015, TANCHJIM has established itself as a measurement-focused manufacturer with dedicated acoustic research capabilities spanning over 40 engineers across design, acoustics, and structural departments.
