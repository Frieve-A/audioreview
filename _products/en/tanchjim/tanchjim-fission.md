## Overview

The TANCHJIM Fission represents the latest evolution in dynamic driver IEM technology, featuring the company's proprietary 5th-generation DMT (Dynamic Driver Technology) with dual-magnetic circuit architecture. This premium in-ear monitor offers an unprecedented 12 distinct sound profiles through its innovative combination of three interchangeable nozzles and four-level adjustable bass filtering system. The Fission integrates cutting-edge DSP processing via USB-C connectivity, parametric EQ controls through TANCHJIM's proprietary application, and premium CNC-machined aluminum construction with sapphire glass accents.
