## Overview

The TFZ KING RS represents the flagship model in TFZ's KING series, incorporating fourth-generation dynamic driver technology with gold diaphragm coating [1]. TFZ (The Fragrant Zither), established in 2015 by former TTpod designers, positions this 239 USD model as their reference quality flagship monitor featuring proprietary Tesla magnetic group technology and dual acoustic chamber design [1]. The KING RS utilizes an 11.4mm dynamic driver with vacuum magnetron sputtering nano-gold coating, detachable 0.78mm 2-pin cable system, and N50 high-strength magnet implementation targeting audiophile applications [1][2].
