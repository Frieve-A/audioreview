## Overview

The MEE audio M6 PRO 2nd Generation represents a refined evolution of the company's flagship professional in-ear monitor, designed specifically for musicians and audio professionals requiring stage-ready monitoring. This second-generation model addresses the original's treble harshness through revised tuning above 2 kHz, delivering smoother midrange accuracy and more natural treble presentation. Founded in 2005 by audio enthusiasts, MEE audio has built a reputation for accessible professional-grade equipment, with the M6 PRO series serving as their core offering in the competitive IEM market. The product maintains the company's philosophy of "Audio Enjoyment for Everyone" through modular design featuring detachable cables, comprehensive accessory packages, and optional customization services.

## Reliability & Support

$$ \Large \text{0.6} $$

MEE audio provides above-average support through manufacturer-direct channels including global email support and comprehensive replacement parts availability. The 1-year limited warranty covers defects in materials and workmanship, supplemented by a unique Lifetime Replacement Program offering half-price replacement for any reason including loss. While cable durability issues have been reported by some users, particularly at connection points, the 2nd generation specifically addresses these concerns through redesigned hardware with reinforced connectors and strain reliefs. The detachable cable system enables repair rather than full unit replacement, effectively extending product lifespan. Parts availability includes earpieces, cables, eartips, and filters with reasonable pricing, though support duration beyond warranty period remains undocumented.
