## Overview

The JBL EON One Compact is a battery-powered portable PA system that integrates a professional-grade speaker, 4-channel digital mixer, and Bluetooth control into a compact 17.6-pound package. Featuring 120W continuous power output, 112 dB maximum SPL, and frequency response extending down to 37.5 Hz through an 8-inch woofer and 1-inch compression driver configuration, the system targets solo performers, DJs, fitness instructors, and presenters requiring portable professional audio capabilities. The unit incorporates JBL's acoustic waveguide technology, professional dbx and Lexicon effects processing, phantom power for condenser microphones, and up to 12 hours of battery operation with comprehensive app-based remote control functionality.
