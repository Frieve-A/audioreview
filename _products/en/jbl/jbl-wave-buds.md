## Overview

The JBL Wave Buds are true wireless earbuds released in February 2023, featuring Bluetooth 5.2 connectivity and IP54 water/dust resistance. Positioned as a budget-oriented product in JBL's consumer lineup, they offer 8 hours of earbud playback with an additional 24 hours from the charging case for 32 hours total battery life. The earbuds support standard Bluetooth codecs (A2DP, AVRCP, HFP) and include JBL Headphones app compatibility with customizable EQ settings. As part of JBL's established audio heritage dating back to 1946, these earbuds represent the company's entry-level offering in the competitive true wireless market.

## Advice

For potential purchasers seeking budget-oriented true wireless earbuds with reliable daily-use features, the JBL Wave Buds offer reasonable functionality at 40 USD. The 32-hour total battery life and IP54 water/dust resistance provide practical advantages for active users, while JBL's established support infrastructure ensures reasonable warranty coverage. However, buyers should consider that the TOZO T10 at 25 USD delivers equivalent core functionality with superior battery life (55 hours) and waterproof rating (IPX8) at a significantly lower price point. The JBL Wave Buds are most suitable for users who prioritize brand recognition and established support infrastructure over cost-effectiveness. Those requiring measured performance verification should await independent third-party measurements before purchase, as current evaluation is limited by insufficient data. Users seeking advanced features such as active noise cancellation, adaptive EQ, or premium codec support should consider higher-tier products. For basic true wireless functionality with reliable build quality, the Wave Buds represent a competent if unremarkable option in the budget category.
