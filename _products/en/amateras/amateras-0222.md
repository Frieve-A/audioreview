## Overview

The AMATERAS 0222 is a transformer-based audio saturation device from Tokyo's AMATERAS, designed for recording, mastering, and mixing applications. Marketed as a "desk treasure box" that delivers "live" saturation effects "that can never be obtained through digital processing," the unit features premium transformers from either LUNDAHL (Sweden) or OEP/Carnhill (UK). The device provides TS phone input/output connectivity via SWITCHCRAFT connectors and includes a red LED indicator to monitor saturation levels. As a custom-made product with approximately 4-week delivery time, it targets recording engineers and musicians seeking analog coloration effects.
