## Overview

The Tannoy Glenair 15 represents the company's Prestige line of floorstanding speakers, featuring the signature 15-inch dual concentric driver technology first developed by Tannoy in 1947. This design integrates a 33mm aluminum alloy dome tweeter positioned within a 380mm treated paper pulp cone woofer, creating a true point-source transducer. The speakers offer 95dB sensitivity with 32Hz-25kHz frequency response and are housed in a twin rear-ported cherry veneer cabinet weighing 45kg each. Originally retailing at 10,000 USD per pair when manufactured until 2014, these speakers are now discontinued with no direct new market successor offering equivalent specifications. The discontinued status limits availability to pre-owned units, though Tannoy's dual concentric technology remains unique in providing true point-source acoustics at this sensitivity level for audiophiles seeking phase-coherent reproduction.
