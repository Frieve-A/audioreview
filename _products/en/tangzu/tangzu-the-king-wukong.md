## Overview

TANGZU THE KING WUKONG represents the company's flagship in-ear monitor, featuring an elaborate tribrid driver configuration with 1 diamond diaphragm dynamic driver, 6 balanced armature drivers, and 4 electrostatic drivers. Housed in a Grade 4 titanium alloy shell and equipped with a 148-core Furukawa silver-copper alloy cable, this 1949 USD IEM positions itself in the ultra-premium market segment. The product incorporates TANGZU's Q-IAO Intelligent Crossover System and draws design inspiration from the legendary Monkey King character, emphasizing both technical sophistication and cultural heritage in its marketing approach.
