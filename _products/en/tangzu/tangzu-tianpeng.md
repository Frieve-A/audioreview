## Overview

TANGZU Tianpeng represents an ambitious flagship IEM featuring an 8-driver tribrid configuration with ceramic piezoelectric bone conduction technology. The design integrates 1x 14.2mm dynamic driver, 6x balanced armature drivers, and 1x ceramic piezoelectric bone conductor housed in medical-grade 3D printed shells. This complex architecture targets specific frequency bands through dedicated driver types, with the bone conduction element aimed at treble enhancement. Priced at 629 USD, the Tianpeng incorporates advanced manufacturing techniques including 4th generation DLP 3D printing and premium 6N OCC silver-plated cabling with 4.4mm balanced connectivity.
