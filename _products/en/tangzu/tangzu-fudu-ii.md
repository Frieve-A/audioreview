## Overview

The TANGZU FUDU II is a hybrid in-ear headphone featuring a three-driver configuration with one titanium diaphragm dynamic driver and two custom balanced armature units. Priced at 99 USD, it incorporates TANGZU's proprietary Q-IAO Lite crossover technology derived from their higher-end systems. The product features CNC-machined brass construction with ebony wood faceplate, representing TANGZU's design philosophy combining Chinese aesthetics with modern audio technology. Key manufacturer specifications include 114.5dB sensitivity at 1kHz, 18Ω impedance, and 20Hz-20kHz frequency range with 0.78mm 2-pin cable interface.
