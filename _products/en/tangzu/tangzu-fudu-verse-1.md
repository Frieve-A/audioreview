## Overview

The Tangzu FUDU VERSE 1 represents Tangzu's collaboration with DIVINUS, featuring a hybrid driver configuration with one 10mm dynamic driver and two custom balanced armature units per side. Priced at 89 USD, this IEM emphasizes "Zen minimalist design philosophy" while targeting audiophiles seeking enhanced bass performance. The product includes premium accessories such as TANG SANCAI Balance eartips, DIVINUS Velvet tips, and a lychee leather carrying pouch. Construction combines 3D-printed transparent cavity with CNC aluminum alloy panels, along with a detachable oxygen-free copper silver-plated cable system using 0.78mm 2-pin connectors.

The FUDU VERSE 1 targets users seeking premium build quality and aesthetic appeal rather than measurable performance advantages.
