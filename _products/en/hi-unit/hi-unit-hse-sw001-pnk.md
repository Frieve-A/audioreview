## Overview

Hi-Unit HSE-SW001-pnk represents the company's first wireless earphone offering, introduced July 2025 as a semi-wireless neckband design monitored by Pierre Nakano from Shirasagi Izen. The product deliberately embraces simplicity with SBC-only codec support, 10mm dynamic drivers, and basic Bluetooth 5.0 connectivity. Priced at 60 USD, it targets users seeking straightforward wireless audio without specification complexity. Key features include 6-hour battery life, water-resistant construction, multipoint connectivity for 2 devices, and Hi-Unit's characteristic emphasis on accessible pricing over premium materials or advanced codec support.

## Advice

The HSE-SW001-pnk suits users prioritizing simplicity and affordable neckband wireless audio over specification maximization. Consider this product if you prefer semi-wireless connectivity over true wireless designs, require basic multipoint functionality, and value straightforward operation without codec complexity. Avoid this product if you need advanced Bluetooth codec support (aptX, LDAC), extended battery life beyond 6 hours, or documented measurement performance for critical listening applications. The 60 USD pricing represents reasonable value within the limited neckband category, though buyers should recognize the SBC-only limitation may constrain audio quality compared to codec-equipped alternatives. Best suited for casual listening where convenience and affordability outweigh maximum performance requirements.
