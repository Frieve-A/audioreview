## Overview

The Hi-Unit HSE-A2000NC is a USB Type-C connected wired earphone featuring active noise cancellation and Hi-Res Audio certification. Developed as the successor to the well-received HSE-A2000, this model incorporates φ8mm high-magnetism drivers with graphene-coated diaphragms and feed-forward ANC technology. The product targets users seeking affordable ANC-equipped wired earphones with modern USB-C connectivity, offering an aluminum machined body construction and comprehensive accessory package including multiple ear tip sizes.
