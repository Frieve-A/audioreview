## Overview

The Hi-Unit HSE-AD03-pnk is a Type-C to 3.5mm DAC adapter featuring CX31993 DAC and HT97220 amplifier chips. Priced at 40 USD, it supports 32bit/384kHz PCM decoding with manufacturer-specified performance including 124dB S/N ratio, 111dB dynamic range, and 0.0018% THD. The product includes a Type-A conversion adapter and targets users seeking audio quality improvements from smartphone and PC audio outputs. Developed under supervision by Yell Nakano.
