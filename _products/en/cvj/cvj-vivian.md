## Overview

The CVJ Vivian is a budget-tier in-ear monitor with pricing varying by configuration, ranging from 18.81 USD to 20.22 USD depending on microphone inclusion and aesthetic variant [2]. This review evaluates the Black-NO-MIC variant at 18.81 USD [2]. The IEM features a dual-magnet, dual-chamber driver design with composite resin diaphragm [1]. Marketed with the tagline "When dark aesthetics meet acoustic innovation," this IEM targets the entry-level market with anime-inspired aesthetics and detachable cable system [1]. CVJ positions the Vivian as offering balanced, slightly warm sound signature developed over 18 months [1], competing in the highly competitive sub-20 USD IEM segment dominated by established players like Moondrop and 7Hz [3].
