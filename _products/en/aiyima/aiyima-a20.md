## Overview

The AIYIMA A20 is a 2.1 channel Class D power amplifier from AIYIMA's "Masterpieces of a Decade" series, delivering 300W per channel at 4 ohms. Built around the TI TPA3255 chipset with Post-Filter Feedback (PFFB) technology, it features both XLR balanced and RCA unbalanced inputs, adjustable high-pass filter (60-200Hz), dedicated subwoofer output, and 12V trigger functionality. The amplifier incorporates LME49720 and NE5532 operational amplifiers with premium components including German WIMA capacitors and Japanese ruby capacitors. Available with regional power supply options (48V 5A or 48V 10A), the A20 targets home theater and stereo applications requiring transparent amplification with subwoofer integration capabilities.
