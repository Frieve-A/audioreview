## Overview

The AIYIMA 800X is an 8-inch powered subwoofer designed to enhance bass response in home audio systems. Built around Texas Instruments' TPA3116D2 Class-D amplifier chip and MVSILICON's BP1048B2 Bluetooth processor, it targets budget-conscious users seeking straightforward bass augmentation with multiple connectivity options [1][2]. The trapezoidal enclosure design features MDF construction with claimed frequency coverage from 30Hz to 300Hz.

## Scientific Validity

$$ \Large \text{0.5} $$

Manufacturer specifications indicate signal-to-noise ratio ≥85dB and harmonic distortion <1% [1]. For subwoofers, the SNR specification falls between sufficient (80dB) and excellent (105dB+) levels. THD is specified as <1%, placing it below the level considered problematic for subwoofers (1% or above). No credible third-party measurement data is available for independent verification. Since evaluation relies solely on manufacturer specifications without independent verification, conservative scoring is applied per policy guidelines. The frequency response specification (30Hz-300Hz) lacks deviation measurements necessary to assess performance against standard ±3dB criteria for subwoofers. Dynamic range and crossover performance specifications are not provided, preventing comprehensive evaluation of measurement criteria applicable to subwoofers.

## Technology Level

$$ \Large \text{0.4} $$

The implementation utilizes standard contemporary components without proprietary technology development. The TPA3116D2 amplifier provides up to 2×50W at 4Ω and represents mature, widely-adopted Class-D technology [2]. BP1048B2 Bluetooth 5.0 processor handles wireless connectivity with standard codec support [3]. No significant technical differentiation or innovation is evident in the design approach, with competitors easily able to replicate the technical implementation using identical or equivalent components.

## Cost-Performance

Target price: 195.99 USD. Equipped with 100W–150W amplification, multiple inputs (RCA, AUX, LFE), and adjustable crossover (50–250Hz). CP = 124.95 USD ÷ 195.99 USD = 0.637, rounded to 0.6.
