## Overview

The AKG K77 is a dynamic closed circumaural headphone featuring 40mm drivers with a frequency response of 18Hz-20kHz. Weighing 190 grams with 32-ohm impedance, this model targets basic personal listening applications. Originally positioned for "professional use" according to marketing materials, the K77 represents AKG's entry-level offering in the closed-back headphone segment. The design includes synthetic grey ear cushions, blue plastic casing, and a thick 2.5-3 meter cable with 3.5mm connector plus 1/4-inch adapter. At its current market price of 59 USD [6], the K77 competes in the budget headphone category against numerous alternatives offering similar or superior specifications.
