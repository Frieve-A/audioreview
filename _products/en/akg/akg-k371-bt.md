## Overview

The AKG K371-BT represents the first headphones engineered to match the AKG Reference Response Acoustics Curve, derived from extensive Harman research involving hundreds of listeners across multiple demographics. Featuring 50mm titanium-coated drivers with Bluetooth 5.0 connectivity and dual wired/wireless operation, the K371-BT targets professional studio applications while offering consumer convenience features. The design incorporates pure oxygen-free copper voice coils and supports AAC/SBC codecs with up to 40 hours of battery life. With an extended frequency response specification of 5Hz-40kHz and measured performance closely adhering to the scientifically-validated Harman target curve, the K371-BT attempts to bridge professional monitoring requirements with wireless functionality at a mid-tier price point.

## Scientific Validity

$$ \Large \text{0.6} $$

Measured performance demonstrates close adherence to the Harman target curve with minimal deviations, as confirmed by credible third-party measurements from Audio Science Review using GRAS 45CA standardized fixtures [1]. Frequency response shows only a slight dip around 3.8-3.9 kHz while maintaining strong sub-bass performance and extended range specification of 5Hz-40kHz [2]. THD performance consistently rated as "very good" and "better than average" across measurement sources, with excellent performance maintained at both 94dB and 104dB SPL levels [1][3]. Sensitivity measures 114dB SPL/V @ 1kHz (manufacturer) and 104.37dB (measured), placing it at transparent level for efficient driving [2][4].
