## Overview

The AKG K99 is a semi-open, over-ear headphone designed for budget-conscious users seeking basic functionality for project studios and rehearsals. Featuring 40mm drivers, a frequency range of 18-22,000 Hz, and 32-ohm impedance, this lightweight 210g headphone offers a self-adjusting headband design with leatherette ear pads. The model includes a 3-meter tangle-free cable with convertible jack connectivity and represents AKG's entry-level approach to professional monitoring headphones.
