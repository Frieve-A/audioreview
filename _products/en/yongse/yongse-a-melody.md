## Overview

The Yongse A-melody is a 4-core earphone cable featuring proprietary Air-shield technology, priced at 280 USD. The cable combines copper-silver alloy conductors (4% silver content) with pure silver conductors, utilizing what the manufacturer claims is a spiral wire guide structure that creates an air insulation layer for external noise blocking and signal path optimization. Available through crowdfunding with various connector options including 0.78mm 2Pin and MMCX terminations with 4.4mm or 2.5mm balanced plugs, the 120cm cable targets audiophiles seeking sound quality improvements through cable replacement.
