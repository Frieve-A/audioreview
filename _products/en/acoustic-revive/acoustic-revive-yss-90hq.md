## Overview

The Acoustic Revive YSS-90HQ is a 900mm speaker stand featuring premium construction with A2017S aerospace-grade aluminum alloy top plate, brass layer, and elliptical steel pillars filled with tourmaline powder and polypropylene. Acoustic Revive, a Japanese audio company established with a focus on precision engineering and traditional craftsmanship, markets this stand as part of their YSS-HQ series anti-vibration speaker stands. The product claims to "enhance the full potential of a speaker without altering its inherent characteristics" through advanced vibration control technology. With a current market price of 565 USD, the YSS-90HQ positions itself in the premium speaker stand segment, targeting audiophiles seeking high-end audio accessories.

## Scientific Validity

$$ \Large \text{0.5} $$

While Acoustic Revive provides no specific measurement data for the YSS-90HQ's vibration control effectiveness, independent research demonstrates that speaker isolation devices can produce measurable acoustic improvements. Bob Katz's AES study using accelerometer measurements found that isolation produced dramatic reduction in delayed energy from 40Hz to above 200Hz compared to wood blocks, with measurable differences including reverberation time changes exceeding 15ms and up to 1dB differences in frequency response and distortion [1]. The study confirmed isolation produces improvement in performance with potential audible benefits. However, without product-specific measurement data confirming the YSS-90HQ achieves similar results, the evaluation remains conservative at the average level between problematic and transparent performance.
