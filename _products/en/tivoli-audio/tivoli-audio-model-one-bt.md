## Overview

The Tivoli Audio Model One BT is a tabletop AM/FM radio with Bluetooth connectivity that combines traditional analog radio design with modern streaming capability. Featuring a single 76.2mm (3-inch) driver, the device supports AM radio (520-1700 kHz), FM radio (87.5-108 MHz), and Bluetooth 5.0. The unit includes auxiliary input/output connections, operates on AC adapter (external power) only, and measures 212.7×133.4×114.3mm with a weight of 1860g. Tivoli Audio positions this product as a lifestyle-oriented device emphasizing classic design aesthetics and simplicity, targeting users who value retro styling over technical advancement.
