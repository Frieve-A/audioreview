## Overview

The Pioneer S-LX70W is a powered subwoofer designed as part of Pioneer's S-LX70 flat panel speaker system, originally released around 2007-2008 to complement Pioneer's KURO television series. This active subwoofer features an 8" cone driver (despite manufacturer specifications claiming 30cm/11.8") powered by a 300W RMS Class D amplifier, with adjustable crossover frequencies ranging from 50 to 200 Hz across eight settings. The system includes Music and Cinema bass modes and supports Dolby Digital 5.1 channel configurations with LFE channel compatibility. The product has been discontinued and is now only available on secondary markets.
