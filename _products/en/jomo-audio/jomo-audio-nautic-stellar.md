## Overview

The Jomo Audio Nautic Stellar represents the company's 10th anniversary flagship, serving as the third model in their Marine Series following the Nautilus and Nautic Gleam [1]. Positioned at 2,600 USD, this universal in-ear monitor features a sophisticated quad-hybrid 8-driver configuration with 5-way crossover system, combining dynamic, balanced armature, planar magnetic, and bone conduction technologies. The product showcases Jomo Audio's emphasis on both acoustic innovation and luxury aesthetics, incorporating 904L stainless steel construction with watch-grade Guilloché engraving. Founded in 2015, Jomo Audio has established itself in the premium IEM market through proprietary technologies and medical-grade manufacturing facilities in Singapore.

## Technology Level

$$ \Large \text{0.9} $$

The Nautic Stellar demonstrates advanced technology implementation through multiple proprietary systems. The quad-hybrid configuration integrates a 10mm silk bio-diaphragm dynamic driver with 72-hour bio-activation treatment, 4 balanced armature units, 6mm nano planar magnetic tweeter, and 2 redesigned Sonion bone conduction drivers [1]. Jomo Audio's proprietary C.S.U. crossover network addresses phase cancellation in multi-driver systems through electronic and acoustic optimization. The company operates medical-grade laboratory facilities with partnerships from established suppliers Knowles and Sonion, demonstrating significant technical expertise accumulation over 10 years. While quad-hybrid configurations are no longer unique in the market, the specific implementation of bone conduction driver modifications and silk bio-diaphragm treatment represents genuine technological innovation with measurable performance impact.
