## Overview

The Fengru CF6 is described as a hybrid in-ear monitor featuring 1 dynamic driver and 2 balanced armature units from budget audio manufacturer Fengru [1]. The AliExpress product page includes pricing, catalog-style bullet text in the item description listing claimed frequency range 20 Hz to 40 kHz, impedance 36 Ω, sensitivity 128 dB, and a distortion line quoted as 3 percent, plus marketing images that include a seller-claimed laboratory frequency response plot [1]. Independent third-party measurements or separate manufacturer documentation are still unavailable [2]. While Fengru produces other budget earphone models like the D08 and EMX500 series, the CF6 has limited presence outside marketplace listings.

## Scientific Validity

$$ \Large \text{0.3} $$

Frequency response is checked against **Harman in-ear 2019** [6] using the seller laboratory plot in the product images [1]; the root-mean-square error versus that target is **about 5.7 dB**, so the mismatch is large. The listing also quotes **3 percent** distortion [1] without test conditions; for earphones that figure is far above the **0.5 percent** problematic band when read as ordinary THD. With no independent third-party data, only seller-provided graphics and text support the assessment, so the score is **0.3**.
