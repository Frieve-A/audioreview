## Overview

The SteelSeries Alias Pro is an XLR gaming microphone system released in October 2023, featuring an integrated stream mixer and comprehensive software suite. The complete kit includes a large 1-inch condenser capsule microphone, dedicated stream mixer with RGB lighting and customizable controls, shock mount, pop filter, and Sonar software with AI-powered noise cancellation. Designed specifically for gaming, streaming, and podcasting applications, it represents SteelSeries' entry into the professional XLR microphone market with dual PC streaming capabilities and parametric EQ functionality.

## Technology Level

$$ \Large \text{0.8} $$

SteelSeries demonstrates significant technical advancement through four years of dedicated development, incorporating over 40 patents including dynamic equalizer technology. The Alias Pro features sophisticated integration of hardware and software, combining a large condenser capsule with an advanced stream mixer containing professional phantom power supply and digital processing capabilities. The inclusion of AI-powered ClearCast noise cancellation, parametric 10-band equalizer, and 360-degree spatial audio processing represents cutting-edge technology implementation. The proprietary Sonar software suite introduces the first parametric EQ specifically designed for gaming applications, with over 150 professionally-crafted audio presets. The dual PC streaming functionality and comprehensive audio routing capabilities demonstrate advanced technical expertise and innovation in the gaming microphone category.
