## Overview

The SIMGOT EA2000 represents an innovative approach to single dynamic driver in-ear monitor design, incorporating a 12mm dual magnetic circuit dynamic driver paired with a 6mm passive radiator in a unique 1DD+1PR configuration [1]. Founded in 2013 with the philosophy "Simple and Elegant," SIMGOT positions the EA2000 as a flagship universal IEM featuring swappable tuning circuits integrated into the cable termination plugs, allowing users to adjust sound characteristics across three different profiles. The 5-axis CNC carved stainless steel construction with high-transparency crystal face panels demonstrates premium build quality, while the 8-core silver-plated OFC cable with 152 total strands provides technical connectivity features. At 319.99 USD, the EA2000 targets the premium IEM segment with Hi-Res Audio certification and an extended frequency response range up to 50kHz [1].
