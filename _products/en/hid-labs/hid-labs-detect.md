## Overview

HID-Labs DETECT is an FPS-optimized gaming earphone launched in September 2025, specifically designed for competitive esports audio. The product features a single Knowles balanced armature driver in an ultra-compact form factor with Japanese domestic manufacturing. Priced at 172 USD, the DETECT targets serious FPS gamers seeking precise sound localization and auditory masking reduction. HID-Labs, founded in 2018 as a gaming device customization company, determined through competitive gaming experience that audio is decisive in winning, leading to this specialized earphone development with premium components including estron Linum cable and Westone Audio eartips.
