## Overview

IsoAcoustics V120 Mount is a studio monitor ceiling and wall isolation mount featuring award-winning vibration isolation technology. The mount accommodates studio monitors up to 40 lbs (18.1 kg) and provides 360 degrees of horizontal rotation plus 90 degrees of vertical tilt adjustment for precise speaker positioning [1]. Constructed from aluminum with M10-1.5 threaded connections, the V120 measures 4.6" x 4.6" x 3.5" (117 x 117 x 90 mm) and adds between 3.5" to 6.3" (90 to 160 mm) depending on mounting configuration. The mount includes a 4" ceiling plate for direct attachment or can connect to wall brackets, unistrut channel, and truss systems using separately sold adapters. IsoAcoustics developed this mount using patent-pending isolation technology designed to decouple studio monitors from wall and ceiling structures, mitigating structure-borne vibrations from exciting supporting surfaces.

## Advice

For professional studio installations requiring monitor isolation and precise positioning control, the IsoAcoustics V120 Mount offers patent-pending vibration isolation technology with comprehensive adjustment capabilities. The product serves studios prioritizing measurable vibration reduction through scientific engineering rather than basic mechanical mounting. Users seeking simple wall or ceiling mounting without isolation requirements will find equivalent positioning functionality through standard speaker mounts at significantly lower cost (Gator GFW-SPK-WM100 at 65.55 USD).
