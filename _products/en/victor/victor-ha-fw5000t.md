## Overview

The Victor HA-FW5000T WOOD master represents Victor's flagship true wireless earbuds, featuring the industry's first hybrid wood driver technology that combines African rosewood with wood pulp (marketed for enhanced acoustic properties; independent verification unavailable) [3]. Released in late November 2025 at approximately 211 USD [2], this premium model incorporates Victor's 18-year heritage in wood driver development with cutting-edge wireless technology. Key innovations include Bluetooth 6.0 with LDAC codec support, active noise cancellation with AI enhancement, K2 Technology for audio upscaling, and comprehensive app-based customization with 10 sound modes [1]. The product targets the premium audiophile wireless market, combining traditional Japanese craftsmanship with modern digital signal processing capabilities.
