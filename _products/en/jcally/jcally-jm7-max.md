## Overview

The JCALLY JM7-MAX is a budget-oriented portable DAC/amplifier featuring the CONEXANT CX31993 DAC chip paired with an SGM8262 operational amplifier. Priced at 19.99 USD, this dongle-style device supports 32-bit/384kHz PCM decoding and delivers 228mW output power at 16Ω impedance with an ultra-low 0.5Ω output impedance. JCALLY, established in 2015 with substantial manufacturing infrastructure, positions this product as an entry-level solution for improving mobile audio quality. The JM7-MAX features a 5-axis CNC machined aluminum chassis with transparent design elements and includes an 8-wire 448-core silver-plated Type-C cable.
