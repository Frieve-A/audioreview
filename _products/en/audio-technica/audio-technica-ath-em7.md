## Overview

The Audio-Technica ATH-EM7 GM represents a 2002-era clip-on headphone design featuring 28mm neodymium magnet drivers housed in forged aluminum alloy construction. Originally priced at 80 USD, this discontinued product offered a unique ear-fitting design with adjustable vertical clip system and spring-loaded swinging hinges. The headphones feature a frequency response of 14-24,000 Hz, 32Ω impedance, and 109 dB SPL/1mW sensitivity, targeting portable audio applications including iPods and computers. While Audio-Technica's engineering heritage from 1962 brought solid construction principles to this design, the product represents technology that has been superseded by modern alternatives offering superior performance and value.
