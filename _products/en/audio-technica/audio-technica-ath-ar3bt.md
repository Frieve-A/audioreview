## Overview

The Audio-Technica ATH-AR3BT is a wireless on-ear headphone featuring 40mm dynamic drivers and Bluetooth connectivity [1][2]. Released by Audio-Technica, a company established in 1962 [4] with over 60 years of audio industry experience, this model targets the mid-range wireless headphone market at 119 USD [5]. The headphones offer Bluetooth 4.1 connectivity with aptX and AAC codec support, 30-hour battery life, and NFC pairing functionality [1][2]. The design features a collapsible frame with swiveling ear cups, synthetic leather padding, and a single sliding control for volume and playback functions [2].
