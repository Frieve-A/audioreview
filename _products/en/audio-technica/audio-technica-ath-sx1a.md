## Overview

The Audio-Technica ATH-SX1a is a discontinued professional broadcast headphone that was released in April 2008. Designed as a compact and lightweight monitoring solution for broadcasting applications, it features a 40mm dynamic driver with "Super Hard Coat Diaphragm," closed-back design for isolation, and professional-grade construction including leather ear and head pads. The single-sided cord design and swiveling ear cups were specifically engineered for broadcast monitoring where convenience and functionality were prioritized over audiophile considerations.
