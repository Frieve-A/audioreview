## Overview

The Audio-Technica AT6900BR is a set of six hybrid speaker insulators designed for small to medium-sized speakers. Each piece features a 3-layer construction combining machined brass with gold plating and Hanenite vibration-damping rubber. With a load capacity of 21kg for 3-point support and individual piece capacity of 7kg, the insulators claim to provide "clear highs with lively mids and lows for a well-balanced sound." Audio-Technica, established in 1962, has built reputation through precision-engineered cartridges, microphones, and headphones, though this accessory represents a departure into passive isolation products without the company's traditional measurement-based validation.

## Technology Level

$$ \Large \text{0.4} $$

The AT6900BR employs traditional mechanical isolation technology through its 3-layer brass-rubber construction. Audio-Technica demonstrates design ownership and applies their accumulated know-how in vibration control from their turntable heritage. However, the approach relies entirely on mature materials and conventional damping principles without cutting-edge innovations. The machined brass with gold plating and Hanenite rubber construction represents competent but outdated technology easily replicable by competitors. While the 18mm height design shows practical consideration for installation convenience, the overall technical approach lacks the sophisticated integration, digital processing, or advanced materials seen in contemporary audio accessories. The technology offers no meaningful competitive advantage duration and represents analog-only thinking without modern engineering approaches.

## Cost-Performance

$$ \Large \text{0.4} $$

The AT6900BR costs approximately 130 USD (converted from 199 AUD market price [6]), providing mechanical vibration isolation for speakers with 21kg load capacity. Equivalent-or-better functionality is available at significantly lower prices. The Auralex MoPAD offers superior 45kg load capacity with proven vibration absorption for 49.99 USD [2], providing isolation for monitor speakers with multiple angle options. Equipped with 100 lbs weight capacity and dense proprietary foam, it provides equivalent vibration isolation function for speaker applications. Cost-performance calculation: 49.99 USD ÷ 130 USD = 0.38, rounded to 0.4. The IsoAcoustics GAIA III provides 32kg capacity at 199.99 USD [3], while Vibrapod isolators offer various load capacities from 2.5-25 lbs per piece for specialized applications [1]. The AT6900BR's premium pricing reflects cosmetic gold plating rather than functional superiority, making it among the least cost-effective options in the speaker isolation category.
