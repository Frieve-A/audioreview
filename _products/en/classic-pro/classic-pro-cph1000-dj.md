## Overview

The CLASSIC PRO CPH1000-DJ represents an entry-level DJ monitor headphone designed for professional DJs and musicians seeking affordable monitoring solutions. Featuring a 57mm dynamic driver with 3500mW maximum input power, the headphones provide essential DJ functionality including 90-degree swivel housing for single-ear monitoring and foldable design for portability. The product includes detachable coiled cable extending from 1.5m to 4.0m, multiple ear pad options, and carrying accessories. Priced at 20 USD (¥2,980), the CPH1000-DJ targets the budget-conscious DJ market segment while maintaining basic professional features necessary for DJ applications. Notably, the product is primarily available through Sound House Japan, with limited global distribution affecting international accessibility.
