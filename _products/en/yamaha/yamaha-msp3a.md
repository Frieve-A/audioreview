## Overview

The Yamaha MSP3A is a compact professional powered monitor speaker designed for small studios, post-production setups, and surround sound reproduction. Released in 2021 as the successor to the MSP3, this 4-inch two-way powered monitor introduces Yamaha's proprietary Twisted Flare Port technology to the MSP series. The speaker features a 22-watt built-in amplifier and weighs only 3.6 kg, making it portable for various monitoring applications. With both professional XLR/TRS (+4 dBu) and consumer RCA (-10 dBu) inputs, tone controls, and the innovative port design, the MSP3A targets professional users requiring accurate monitoring in compact form factor.

## Technology Level

$$ \Large \text{0.6} $$

The MSP3A incorporates Twisted Flare Port technology, which represents a notable advancement in port design. However, the overall technological sophistication is moderate. The 3-inch woofer with 1-inch tweeter configuration is standard for near-field monitoring applications. While the integration of professional-grade connectivity (XLR/TRS balanced inputs) with consumer-friendly features (RCA inputs) shows thoughtful engineering, the technology level is not exceptional compared to other professional monitors in this price range. The design relies solely on analog/mechanical implementation without digital signal processing integration, and the Twisted Flare Port, while innovative, does not represent groundbreaking technology that would significantly differentiate it from competitors.

## Advice

For professional users requiring accurate monitoring with both consumer and professional connectivity in compact form factor, the MSP3A represents a good choice in its price segment. The combination of Twisted Flare Port technology, dual input capabilities, and Yamaha's proven "Natural Sound" philosophy makes it suitable for small studios, post-production facilities, and surround monitoring applications. However, buyers should note limited availability with current backorders extending into 2025. Users seeking better cost-performance might consider the JBL 305P MkII at 318.00 USD per pair, which offers larger drivers and better low-frequency extension. The MSP3A's 351.98 USD pricing per pair reflects fair value for professional-grade monitoring with proprietary port technology, making it recommended for users prioritizing compact form factor and Twisted Flare Port technology over enhanced low-frequency performance.
