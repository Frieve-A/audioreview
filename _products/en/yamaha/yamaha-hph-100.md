## Overview

The Yamaha HPH-100 are closed-back dynamic headphones designed for comfortable listening and digital instrument monitoring. Featuring 40mm dynamic drivers in a closed-back configuration, these headphones target entry-level users seeking basic audio reproduction for both recorded media and digital piano practice. Available in black and white variants, they include a 90-degree swivel mechanism for ear angle adjustment, single 2-meter cable configuration, and dual 3.5mm/6.3mm connectors. With 46-ohm impedance and 20Hz-20kHz frequency response, the HPH-100 represents Yamaha's conventional approach to affordable headphone design, emphasizing ergonomic comfort and practical functionality over technical innovation.
