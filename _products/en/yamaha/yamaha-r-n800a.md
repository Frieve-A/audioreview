## Overview

The Yamaha R-N800A is a network receiver that combines traditional stereo amplification with advanced digital functionality. Delivering 100W + 100W into 8 ohms, it features Yamaha's proprietary YPAO room correction system, ESS SABRE ES9080Q DAC with native DSD 11.2 MHz and PCM 384 kHz support, and comprehensive network streaming capabilities including Wi-Fi, Ethernet, AirPlay 2, and Bluetooth connectivity. The unit incorporates ToP-ART technology for signal path optimization and Pure Direct mode for minimal signal interference.

## Scientific Validity

$$ \Large \text{0.5} $$

The R-N800A demonstrates competent measured performance within available manufacturer specifications. Frequency response of 20-20 kHz ±0.5 dB meets transparent level criteria exactly, with extended response of 10 Hz-100 kHz ±1.0 dB exceeding typical requirements. Signal-to-noise ratio of 100 dB (Pure Direct ON) and THD of 0.07% at rated output fall between problematic and transparent thresholds according to measurement criteria. Conservative evaluation applied due to reliance on manufacturer specifications without independent third-party verification.
