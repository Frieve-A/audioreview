## Overview

The Yamaha YH-4000 represents the company's premium open-back headphone offering, inspired by their flagship YH-5000SE model. Featuring Yamaha's proprietary Orthodynamic planar magnetic drivers, the YH-4000 incorporates advanced materials including a magnesium body frame, aerospace-inspired triaxial woven mesh, and ultra-thin diaphragms with double-sided voice coils. Hand-assembled in Yamaha's Kakegawa factory, these headphones embody the company's "True Sound" philosophy, targeting audiophiles seeking high-end audio reproduction. At 320 grams and priced at 2,499 USD, the YH-4000 enters the competitive premium headphone market with significant technological sophistication.

## Technology Level

$$ \Large \text{0.9} $$

The YH-4000 demonstrates exceptional technology implementation through Yamaha's proprietary Orthodynamic planar magnetic technology, representing significant modernization of their 1970s HP-1 heritage. The design incorporates sophisticated materials including magnesium frame construction for optimal strength-to-weight ratio, triaxial woven mesh inspired by aerospace applications, and precision-engineered PET mesh filters for sound pressure regulation. The ultra-thin diaphragm with double-sided voice coils represents advanced planar magnetic implementation, while the arch-shaped internal reflector demonstrates acoustic engineering sophistication. Hand-assembly in Yamaha's dedicated Kakegawa facility ensures manufacturing precision. The integration of proprietary driver technology with cutting-edge materials places this product at the forefront of contemporary headphone technology, earning recognition for innovation and technical achievement.
