## Overview

The Yamaha HPH-150 are open-air dynamic headphones specifically designed for listening to digital musical instruments. Featuring 40mm rear-vented drivers, these headphones aim to provide neutral sound reproduction for electronic piano and synthesizer monitoring. Available in black and white variants, they include velour ear pads and a 90-degree swivel mechanism for comfort during extended practice sessions. With a 20Hz-20kHz frequency response and 48-ohm impedance, they represent Yamaha's entry-level approach to specialized instrument monitoring headphones.
