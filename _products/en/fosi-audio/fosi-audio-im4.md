## Overview

The Fosi Audio IM4 Open-Back In-Ear Monitor represents an innovative approach in the IEM market, featuring a rare open-back design combined with premium materials at a competitive 99.99 USD price point [1]. Each 7g earpiece is enclosed in precision CNC aluminum housing with a 10mm beryllium-coated diaphragm and N52 dual-magnet system [1]. The product includes interchangeable sound nozzles (brass and aluminum), multiple eartip options, and detachable OFC silver-plated cables with both 3.5mm and 4.4mm terminations [1]. Fosi Audio, known primarily for amplifiers, has expanded into personal audio with this unique offering that challenges conventional IEM design paradigms [1].

## Cost-Performance

$$ \Large \text{1.0} $$

This site evaluates based solely on functionality and measured performance values, without considering driver types or configurations. The IM4 currently stands as the world's cheapest true open-back IEM with equivalent or better functionality and performance at 99.99 USD [1]. While semi-open IEMs like the KZ PR2 exist at lower prices, they do not provide true open-back functionality - the KZ PR2 features only semi-open vents and "faux semi-open back mesh" rather than genuine open-back acoustics [5]. True open-back IEMs under 100 USD are extremely rare in the market [5], with most alternatives such as the Audeze iSINE series priced significantly higher at 300+ USD. Since no cheaper equivalent true open-back IEM exists with comparable functionality and specifications, the cost-performance evaluation yields the maximum score of 1.0.

## Advice

The Fosi Audio IM4 represents an exceptional choice for users seeking genuine open-back IEM experience with high-quality materials and construction. The 99.99 USD investment delivers a 10mm beryllium-coated diaphragm with N52 dual-magnet system, CNC aluminum construction, and comprehensive customization options that distinguish it from semi-open alternatives [1]. The open-back design provides distinct acoustic characteristics compared to conventional sealed IEMs, making it ideal for users who prefer spacious soundstage presentation in quiet environments. The interchangeable nozzles and multiple eartip options allow fine-tuning to personal preferences [1]. Potential buyers should note the extreme rarity of true open-back IEMs under 100 USD and the lack of comprehensive measurement data for objective performance evaluation [5]. The product suits users prioritizing genuine open-back functionality and premium build quality. Consider this option if seeking the most affordable entry point into true open-back IEM technology while valuing premium materials and construction quality.
