## Overview

The Xiaomi Redmi Buds 6 Lite represents Xiaomi's entry-level true wireless stereo earbuds, positioned in the ultra-budget segment at approximately 20 USD [2]. Released in 2024, these earbuds feature 12.4mm titanium-diaphragm drivers [2], hybrid active noise cancellation rated up to 40dB [2], and an impressive 38-hour total battery life [1] with the charging case. The product targets cost-conscious consumers seeking essential wireless earbud functionality with active noise cancellation capabilities at an accessible price point.

## Technology Level

$$ \Large \text{0.6} $$

The Redmi Buds 6 Lite demonstrates contemporary technology adoption with Bluetooth 5.3 [1], AAC codec support [2], and Google Fast Pair integration [2] representing current industry standards. Advanced functional integration includes hybrid active noise cancellation with AI-powered wind noise reduction capable of handling wind speeds up to 6m/s [2], alongside a four-microphone array for enhanced noise processing. The Xiaomi Earbuds app [2] provides customizable EQ settings, while quick charging functionality delivers 2 hours of playback from a 10-minute charge [2]. The combination of titanium-diaphragm drivers [2], IP54 environmental protection [2], and software-based audio processing reflects effective technology integration suitable for the price segment.

At 20 USD [2], the Redmi Buds 6 Lite delivers hybrid active noise cancellation (40dB) [2], 38-hour total battery life [1], Bluetooth 5.3 connectivity [1], AAC codec support [2], customizable EQ via app [2], and IP54 protection [2].
