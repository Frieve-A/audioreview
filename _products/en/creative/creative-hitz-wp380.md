## Overview

The Creative Hitz WP380 was a wireless Bluetooth on-ear headphone released in October 2013, featuring Creative's proprietary ShareMe technology that enabled two headsets to wirelessly share audio from a single source device [1]. The product combined Bluetooth 3.0 connectivity with NFC one-touch pairing and supported high-quality audio codecs including aptX, AAC, and FastStream. Priced at 100 USD, the WP380 targeted consumers seeking wireless convenience with social audio sharing capabilities. The headphone included 34mm neodymium drivers, multipoint connection support, and 12-hour battery life with wired backup capability. Creative positioned this model as a mid-range wireless solution with practical features rather than premium audiophile positioning. The product is now discontinued and no longer available for purchase.
