## Overview

The Creative Aurvana Live! SE represents Creative Technology's approach to affordable audiophile headphones, featuring 40mm Foster bio-cellulose drivers with neodymium magnets in a closed-back over-ear design. Originally launched as a revival of the popular Aurvana Live series, this model incorporates Super X-Fi certification for spatial audio compatibility with Creative's proprietary amplification systems. Positioned in Creative's mid-range lineup at 85 USD, it targets music enthusiasts seeking realistic audio reproduction while maintaining budget-conscious pricing. The product utilizes the established Foster OEM platform that has appeared in various forms across multiple manufacturers, combined with Creative's acoustic tuning and modern spatial audio processing technology.

## Scientific Validity

$$ \Large \text{0.4} $$

Third-party measurements from DIY-Audio-Heaven and Stereophile reveal mixed performance characteristics [1][2]. At 90dB SPL, THD reaches about 8% at 60Hz, and distortion remains high enough to be called "on the high side" even at 80dB SPL/1kHz [1]. Frequency response also shows a warm tilt from 200Hz to 2kHz with treble irregularities, and passive isolation is reported as limited below 1kHz [1]. Resonance frequency is measured at 50Hz [1]. In short, the available data indicate acceptable tonal balance for price, but they do not support a transparent-level technical result.

## Technology Level

$$ \Large \text{0.7} $$

The Creative Aurvana Live! SE combines mature OEM driver technology with cutting-edge proprietary spatial audio processing. Core transduction utilizes Foster's 40mm bio-cellulose diaphragm technology with neodymium magnets, representing established driver design from 2011. However, Creative's integration of Super X-Fi AI-based HRTF processing demonstrates advanced technological development, incorporating personalized spatial audio algorithms with dedicated processing silicon. The bio-cellulose diaphragm technology provides sound velocity characteristics comparable to aluminum while maintaining warm acoustic properties, though this represents mature rather than cutting-edge driver technology. Super X-Fi certification indicates compatibility with Creative's proprietary spatial audio ecosystem, representing significant advancement in headphone-amplifier integration through computational audio processing.

## Cost-Performance

$$ \Large \text{0.7} $$

This site evaluates based solely on functionality and measured performance values, without considering driver types or configurations.

CP = 59 USD ÷ 85 USD = 0.6941

Current market price of the review target is 85 USD [6]. The cheapest equivalent comparator identified is Audio-Technica ATH-M20x at 59 USD [5]. It is selected because it provides the same user-facing basics (wired, closed-back, over-ear, studio monitoring use) and has third-party measured evidence [4]. On numeric performance, Aurvana Live! SE shows about 8% THD at 60Hz/90dB SPL [1], while ATH-M20x is reported at 0.18% weighted THD around 90-100dB SPL [4]. For passive isolation, ATH-M20x reaches about 35dB attenuation around 8kHz [4], while Aurvana Live! SE is reported as weak below 1kHz [1]. Based on these measured points and lower price, CP is recalculated to 0.6941 and rounded to 0.7.

## Reliability & Support

$$ \Large \text{0.3} $$

Creative provides 12-month manufacturer warranty coverage for hardware defects, accompanied by global support infrastructure including online portal, live chat, and RMA system. However, documented reliability concerns significantly impact long-term durability expectations. Reports indicate frequent cable failures due to thin construction and poor connector quality, with non-replaceable cable design creating inherent weakness. Plastic headband components demonstrate fragility with documented failures within 3-4 months of use. Speaker failures occurring after warranty expiration represent additional reliability concerns, along with driver buzzing issues around 30Hz frequencies. The minimal warranty period and documented structural weaknesses in critical components substantially limit reliability expectations compared to industry standards.

## Rationality of Design Philosophy

$$ \Large \text{0.7} $$

Creative employs a rational cost-performance optimization strategy that effectively allocates resources toward functional audio performance rather than premium materials or aesthetics. The design philosophy demonstrates scientific rationality by utilizing proven OEM technology from Foster while integrating modern computational audio processing through Super X-Fi spatial audio algorithms. Cost allocation prioritizes driver quality and acoustic performance over build materials, representing efficient engineering for price-performance optimization. The integration of AI-based HRTF processing with dedicated silicon demonstrates advancement beyond traditional analog approaches. However, conservative reliance on established OEM partnerships rather than innovative driver development limits technological progression, and documented reliability compromises suggest insufficient attention to long-term durability considerations in the design process.

## Advice

The Creative Aurvana Live! SE offers competitive measured acoustic performance and advanced spatial audio processing capabilities at an aggressive price point, making it suitable for users prioritizing audio quality over build durability. The combination of Foster bio-cellulose drivers with Super X-Fi certification provides functional value for users within Creative's ecosystem. However, documented reliability concerns including cable failures and plastic component fragility require consideration for long-term use scenarios. Users requiring durable construction or extended warranty coverage should consider alternatives despite higher pricing. The product represents optimal cost-performance for users accepting reliability limitations in exchange for acoustic performance and spatial audio capabilities.
