## Overview

The dbx 215s is a professional dual-channel 15-band graphic equalizer designed for touring, studio, and audio installation applications [1]. Part of dbx's 2-Series line, it features 1/3-octave constant Q frequency bands with switchable ±6dB or ±12dB boost/cut ranges and comprehensive professional connectivity. The unit incorporates an internal toroidal transformer and maintains dbx's established reputation for signal processing excellence in professional audio applications.
