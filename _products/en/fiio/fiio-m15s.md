## Overview

The FiiO M15S represents the company's high-end Digital Audio Player positioned below the flagship M17 model, featuring the ES9038PRO DAC chip and innovative dual power supply architecture. This portable device targets audiophiles seeking high-resolution audio playback with comprehensive format support including 32-bit/384kHz PCM and DSD256. The M15S incorporates advanced engineering solutions including five gain levels, desktop mode functionality, and Android 10 with streaming service integration. FiiO has established itself as a prominent manufacturer in the portable audio market for over 18 years since its founding in 2007, consistently delivering measurement-focused products that prioritize objective performance metrics.
