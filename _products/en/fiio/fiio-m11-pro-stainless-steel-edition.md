## Overview

The FiiO M11 Pro Stainless Steel Edition was a premium limited edition variant of FiiO's flagship digital audio player, originally released in late 2019 with delivery beginning January 2020. Distinguished by its 316 stainless steel construction and strictly limited production of 1505 uniquely numbered units (NO. 0002-1506, with NO. 0001 officially reserved), this discontinued model represents a historical collectible in portable audio. Built around dual AK4497EQ DAC chips and THX AAA-78 amplification technology, the device targeted audiophiles seeking high-resolution portable audio playback with comprehensive connectivity options. The stainless steel edition combined precision machinery with handicraft finishing, featuring laser engraving and individual numbering that positioned it as both an audio device and collectible item within FiiO's product hierarchy during its limited production period.
