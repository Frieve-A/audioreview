## Overview

The FiiO M17 represents the pinnacle of portable digital audio player engineering, combining desktop-caliber internals with portable functionality. This flagship device features dual ES9038PRO DAC chips—the same desktop-grade, 8-channel implementations found in high-end stationary equipment—paired with THX's exclusive AAA-788+ amplification technology adapted specifically for portable use. With up to 3W output power per channel and comprehensive format support extending to 384kHz/32-bit PCM and DSD256 for local playback (768kHz/32-bit PCM and DSD512 via USB DAC), the M17 targets serious audiophiles requiring uncompromised performance in a portable package. The device incorporates cutting-edge components including NDK femtosecond crystal oscillators, ultra-low noise LT3045 LDO regulators, and a massive 9200mAh battery with dual power supply architecture.

## Scientific Validity

$$ \Large \text{0.7} $$

The M17 demonstrates exceptional measured performance in available manufacturer specifications. THD+N measures ≤0.0007% at 1kHz/32Ω, significantly exceeding the transparent level threshold of 0.01%. SNR reaches ≥120-122dB (A-weighted) depending on output mode, surpassing the 105dB transparent benchmark. Output impedance specifications of <1Ω (single-ended) and <2Ω (balanced) ensure excellent headphone compatibility. The device delivers substantial power output: ≥3W into 32Ω in balanced output (both Over-ear headphone mode and Enhanced over-ear headphone mode), with ≥500mW and ≥250mW respectively into 300Ω loads in Enhanced and standard modes. Noise floor measures ≤3.5μV (A-weighted), indicating excellent low-level signal handling. However, critical measurements including frequency response deviation, crosstalk, IMD, and dynamic range are not publicly available from independent third-party sources. Due to insufficient data from credible third-party measurements and reliance primarily on manufacturer specifications, conservative adjustment of 0.1 toward 0.5 is applied per policy [1].

## Technology Level

$$ \Large \text{0.8} $$

The M17 showcases advanced engineering with proprietary patent technology through its THX AAA-788+ amplifier implementation—an exclusive desktop adaptation developed collaboratively with THX specifically for portable applications. Design ownership is evident in the sophisticated dual power supply architecture and desktop-grade component integration. Technology recency is demonstrated through NDK femtosecond crystal oscillators providing cutting-edge clock precision, latest-generation dual ES9038PRO implementation with 8-channel design per channel, and ultra-low noise LT3045 LDO regulators. The implementation approach represents desirable technology likely to influence competitor designs, with high technical expertise required for successful integration of desktop-class components in portable form factor. Advanced power management allows dual supply operation (battery and DC), while sophisticated signal path design maintains performance across multiple gain settings from -6dB to +16dB [2][3].

## Advice

The FiiO M17 suits audiophiles prioritizing uncompromised sound quality in portable applications, particularly those requiring substantial headphone driving capability up to 3W output power. The device excels with high-impedance headphones and demanding planar magnetic drivers benefiting from its desktop-grade amplification. Consider the M17 when desktop-quality audio reproduction is essential during mobile listening, especially for critical listening sessions requiring maximum fidelity. The dual power supply architecture optimizes both portable battery operation and enhanced DC-powered performance for stationary use. However, evaluate whether the 610g weight and substantial size align with your portability requirements, as the device prioritizes performance over compact dimensions. The premium pricing is justified by exceptional measured specifications and advanced technology implementation, making it suitable for users prioritizing audio quality over cost considerations.
