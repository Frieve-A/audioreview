## Overview

The FiiO BTR11 represents a significant achievement in affordable wireless audio, being the first product in its price range to support the LDAC Bluetooth codec. This compact 13-gram device combines Bluetooth 5.3 connectivity with an integrated DAC and headphone amplifier, targeting music enthusiasts seeking wireless audio quality without premium pricing. Built around the BES2700IHC Bluetooth chip and HT97220 amplifier, the BTR11 delivers 45mW output power with measured specifications including ≥101dB signal-to-noise ratio and <0.008% total harmonic distortion. With 15-hour battery life using standard codecs, the BTR11 establishes a new entry point for high-quality wireless audio accessibility.

## Advice

For budget-conscious users seeking wireless audio functionality, the BTR11 offers exceptional value by providing LDAC codec support previously unavailable in its price range. Ideal for users with LDAC-compatible Android devices. Consider battery life requirements, as LDAC usage reduces operation time to 8.5 hours compared to 15 hours with standard codecs. Avoid fast charging adapters due to documented compatibility issues; use standard 5V/1A chargers as specified. For users requiring balanced output, USB DAC functionality, or advanced app control features, consider higher-priced alternatives like the BTR5 or Qudelix-5K. The BTR11 succeeds as an entry point to high-quality wireless audio for users prioritizing codec support and basic functionality over advanced features.
