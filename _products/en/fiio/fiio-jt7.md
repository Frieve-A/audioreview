## Overview

The FiiO JT7 represents the company's effort to democratize planar magnetic technology through an affordable, lightweight open-back headphone design. Featuring a 95×86mm planar driver with 3µm ultra-thin diaphragm and proprietary F.E.S. (FiiO Elastic System) technology, the JT7 weighs just 318g while maintaining Hi-Res Audio certification for 7Hz-40kHz frequency response. At 119.99 USD, it targets budget-conscious audiophiles seeking planar magnetic performance without the typical weight and cost penalties. The foldable design with detachable cables positions it as a portable solution in a traditionally desktop-focused category.

## Technology Level

$$ \Large \text{0.9} $$

The JT7 demonstrates significant technological advancement through proprietary F.E.S. (FiiO Elastic System) featuring embossed folds on the 3µm diaphragm for enhanced bass dynamics and reduced distortion [1]. The dual-sided N52 neodymium magnetic array with lightweight ferrite construction achieves exceptional weight reduction to 318g compared to typical 400-500g planar headphones. FiiO's in-house driver design incorporating multi-dimensional diaphragm textures and symmetrical driving force represents desirable technology that addresses known planar weight limitations while maintaining performance standards.
