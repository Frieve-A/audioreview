## Overview

The intime 轟 (Go) Mark II represents a premium Japanese in-ear monitor featuring hybrid driver configuration with proprietary VST2 ceramic tweeter technology [2]. Developed by Ozeid Co., Ltd. under founder Yoshiyuki Watanabe's 35+ years expertise in piezoelectric materials, this 219 USD IEM combines graphene/titanium-coated dynamic woofer with 3rd generation VST2 ceramic tweeter in hand-finished anodized titanium alloy housing [3]. Manufactured in Japan's renowned Tsubame-Sanjo region, it targets young audiophiles seeking high-resolution sound with detachable cable connectivity (MMCX/Pentaconn Ears options) [2].
