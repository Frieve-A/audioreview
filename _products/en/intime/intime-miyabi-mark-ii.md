## Overview

The Intime Miyabi Mark II (雅) represents a premium Japanese in-ear monitor featuring advanced hybrid driver configuration with proprietary 3rd generation VST2 (Vertical Support Tweeter) ceramic technology [1]. Developed by founder Yoshiyuki Watanabe utilizing over 35 years of ceramic device expertise spanning cellphone speakers, fish finders, and submarine sonar applications, this 219 USD IEM combines 10mm titanium-coated dynamic woofer with 9mm multi-layer ceramic tweeter in titanium alloy housing [2]. Positioned as an "elegant allrounder" with flatter tuning for wide genre compatibility, the Miyabi Mark II features MMCX detachable cables, Pentaconn Ears connectivity, and premium Japanese craftsmanship targeting high-resolution audio enthusiasts [3].
