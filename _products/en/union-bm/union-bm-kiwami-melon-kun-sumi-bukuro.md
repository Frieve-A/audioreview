## Overview

The Union BM Kiwami Melon-kun Sumi-bukuro is an audio accessory consisting of a drawstring bag containing approximately 1kg of Laos binchotan charcoal and 400g of mineral blend including natural crystal, wheat stone, medical stone, and black tourmaline. Priced at 24 USD, the product claims to provide electromagnetic wave countermeasures and audio quality improvement when placed near speakers or cable concentration areas. Union BM, a small Japanese company founded in 2003, developed this as part of their "Kiwami" series with the motto of achieving "sound quality beyond one's means at an affordable price."

The claims about charcoal and mineral blends affecting electromagnetic waves and audio quality lack scientific measurement verification within audible thresholds. This represents a classic case of high-priced products claiming audible improvement effects without scientific evidence.
