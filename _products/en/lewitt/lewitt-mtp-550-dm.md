## Overview

The LEWITT MTP 550 DM is a dynamic handheld vocal microphone designed for live performance applications. Launched by Austrian audio company LEWITT, this cardioid microphone features a zinc die-cast enclosure, rubber shock mounting, and built-in wind/pop filtering [1]. With a frequency range of 60Hz-16kHz and 2mV/Pa sensitivity, it targets professional vocal applications where feedback resistance and handling noise reduction are priorities [1]. The microphone incorporates conventional dynamic transducer technology with standard 3-pin XLR connectivity and weighs 381g [1].
