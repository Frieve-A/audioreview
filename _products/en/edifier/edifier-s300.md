## Overview

The Edifier S300 is a premium tabletop wireless speaker launched in January 2025, combining retro aesthetic with modern connectivity technology. Features dual 1.25" titanium dome tweeters, 5.5" aluminum mid-bass driver, and 80W RMS total output with comprehensive wireless integration including Bluetooth 5.4 with LDAC codec support and Apple AirPlay 2 over dual-band Wi-Fi. The speaker includes 24-bit/96kHz digital signal processing, two-way active crossover, and Hi-Res Audio certification, targeting audiophiles seeking wireless convenience.

## Technology Level

$$ \Large \text{0.5} $$

The S300 employs established technologies including TAS5822M Class-D amplifiers providing 80W RMS output and 24-bit/96kHz DSP processing with two-way active crossover implementation. Connectivity features Bluetooth 5.4 with LDAC codec support and dual-band Wi-Fi with AirPlay 2 integration. Driver technology utilizes standard titanium dome tweeters with neodymium magnets and aluminum diaphragm mid-bass driver. Design represents in-house integration of mature technologies rather than technological advancement.

## Cost-Performance

$$ \Large \text{1.0} $$

Comprehensive investigation found no products providing equivalent-or-better combination of user-facing functions and measured performance at lower price. Required equivalence criteria: 80W RMS output, S/N ratio ≥85dB(A), Bluetooth with LDAC codec, Apple AirPlay 2, 24-bit/96kHz DSP processing, and mobile app control with multi-room capability. Edifier M330 II (138 USD) offers LDAC and AirPlay 2 but delivers only 60W RMS continuous output, failing the ≥80W requirement [6]. Edifier MR5 (349.99 USD) provides 110W RMS with LDAC but lacks AirPlay 2; adding WiiM Mini (89 USD) for AirPlay 2 yields 438.99 USD total, exceeding S300 price [7][8]. Studio monitors with equivalent measured performance require additional LDAC receiver and AirPlay 2 streamer, resulting in combinations exceeding 400 USD. No cheaper equivalent-or-better product exists.
