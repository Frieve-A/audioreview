## Overview

The Edifier G2000 represents the company's mid-tier gaming speaker offering under the HECATE gaming brand [1]. Released before 2021, this 2.0 desktop speaker system targets gaming enthusiasts requiring compact speakers with visual appeal. The G2000 features 2.75-inch composite fiber drivers delivering 16W total RMS power, complemented by 12 customizable RGB lighting effects and three distinct sound modes for gaming, movies, and music [1]. Current market price of 149.99 USD positions it between entry-level gaming speakers and premium desktop monitors [2]. Edifier's established presence in consumer audio since 1996 provides brand recognition, though the G2000 represents incremental improvement over the company's G1000 rather than breakthrough innovation.

## Reliability & Support

$$ \Large \text{0.4} $$
