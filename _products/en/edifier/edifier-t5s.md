## Overview

The Edifier T5S is a compact 8-inch powered subwoofer launched in September 2025, targeting users seeking flexible bass integration across desktop and small room audio systems. Edifier, established in 1996, has built a reputation for consumer-oriented audio products. The T5S features a slim vertical design with 70W RMS Class-D amplification, built-in DSP for low-pass filtering and phase alignment, and dual RCA connectivity for seamless integration. At 199.99 USD, it positions itself as a mid-range option emphasizing space efficiency and aesthetic discretion while providing deep bass extension down to 35Hz.

## Advice

The T5S suits users prioritizing space efficiency and aesthetic integration over maximum acoustic performance. Its compact vertical design and DSP integration make it effective for desktop systems and small room applications where discretion is valued. The dual RCA connectivity and auto standby features provide convenient integration with existing setups. However, potential buyers seeking maximum value should consider that comprehensive measurement data remains unavailable, limiting performance verification. Users requiring high SPL capability or maximum extension should evaluate larger alternatives. The 199.99 USD pricing represents fair value given the feature set, though measurement transparency could be improved.
