## Overview

The CCZ TianGong CZ10 is a hybrid in-ear monitor featuring a 1 dynamic driver + 4 balanced armature configuration in an all-metal zinc alloy housing. Positioned as a "precision in motion" product with gear-inspired aesthetics, the CZ10 targets users seeking multi-driver performance in a durable package. The earphones feature detachable 2-pin cables and 3.5mm connectivity, with specifications including 16Ω impedance and 102dB sensitivity. CCZ markets the product under their "Born for HiFi Sound" philosophy, emphasizing both sound quality and visual design in their affordable product lineup.

## Technology Level

The CZ10 employs contemporary hybrid technology with a dual magnetic PET diaphragm system in the 10mm dynamic driver and precision crossover technology for the 4BA configuration. The implementation represents in-house design by CCZ with all-metal zinc alloy construction and OFC silver-plated cabling.
