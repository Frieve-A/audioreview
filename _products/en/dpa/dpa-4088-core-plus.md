## Overview

The DPA 4088 Core Plus represents DPA Microphones' flagship directional headset microphone, incorporating their latest CORE+ distortion reduction technology. This professional-grade microphone targets live performance, broadcast, and theatrical applications where feedback rejection and audio clarity are paramount. The 4088 Core Plus features a cardioid pickup pattern, IP58 water and dust resistance certification, and multiple termination options including MicroLock, LEMO, TA4F, Mini-XLR, and Mini-Jack connectors. Available in black, brown, and beige colors with standard and small sizing options, this microphone represents DPA's commitment to precision engineering and manufacturing excellence through their 200-step assembly process with 15-point calibration system.

## Scientific Validity

$$ \Large \text{0.4} $$

The DPA 4088 Core Plus demonstrates mixed performance when evaluated against objective measurement criteria for professional microphones. Maximum SPL capability of 144 dB significantly exceeds the excellent threshold of 140 dB, placing it among the highest-performing microphones in this specification [1]. Dynamic range of 111 dB surpasses the transparent level requirement of 105 dB, indicating superior signal-to-noise performance [1]. THD specification of less than 1% up to 137 dB SPL demonstrates reasonable distortion control within acceptable professional standards [1]. However, the equivalent noise level of typically 26 dB(A) (maximum 28 dB(A)) exceeds the problematic threshold of 20 dB(A) established for professional microphones, representing a significant limitation in scientific validity [3]. This noise floor performance directly impacts the microphone's ability to capture clean audio in professional applications, particularly in quiet environments where low self-noise is critical.
