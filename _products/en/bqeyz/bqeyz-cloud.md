## Overview

The BQEYZ Cloud is a single dynamic driver in-ear monitor featuring a 10mm LCP (Liquid Crystal Polymer) diaphragm paired with a 6.8mm passive radiator design. Priced at 169 USD, this IEM represents BQEYZ's approach to atmospheric vocal tuning with what they describe as balanced frequency response and clean vocal separation. The Cloud features a detachable 0.78mm 2-pin cable system with multiple connector options (2.5mm, 3.5mm, 4.4mm) and includes a silver-plated cable with gold-plated connectors. The housing is precision-machined from aluminum alloy using German 5-axis CNC manufacturing processes.

## Technology Level

$$ \Large \text{0.7} $$

The Cloud demonstrates solid contemporary technology implementation through its LCP diaphragm and passive radiator design. BQEYZ engineers developed the cavity structure to optimize airflow distribution, representing in-house design capabilities backed by 20 years of manufacturing experience. The passive radiator concept shows technical competence and represents technology that other manufacturers desire to adopt, as evidenced by patent documentation detailing enhanced back cavity resonance and improved bass response [2]. The precision-machined aluminum housing from German 5-axis CNC processes and silver-plated cable with gold-plated connectors indicate quality manufacturing standards. However, the purely analog/mechanical approach without digital integration limits the technology advancement in the current landscape where software-based solutions provide superior cost-effectiveness.

## Cost-Performance

$$ \Large \text{0.1} $$

This site evaluates based solely on functionality and measured performance values, without considering driver types or configurations. The BQEYZ Cloud at 169 USD faces direct competition from alternatives offering equivalent or superior functionality and measured performance at lower prices. The Moondrop Chu II at 19 USD provides equivalent-or-better specifications with superior 119dB/Vrms@1kHz sensitivity compared to Cloud's 110dB, equivalent 18Ω±15% impedance versus Cloud's 32Ω, effective frequency response range of 20Hz-20kHz (IEC60318-4, 3dB), low distortion (<0.05%) confirmed by third-party measurements, and detachable 0.78mm 2-pin cable system [4][5]. Equipped with superior sensitivity, equivalent impedance, effective frequency response range, low distortion, and equivalent connectivity features, the Chu II represents equivalent-or-better functionality and measured performance. The 4.4mm balanced connection can be addressed with minimal adapter (approximately 10 USD), but even without adapter normalization, the Chu II provides equivalent-or-better measured performance at base price. CP = 19 USD ÷ 169 USD = 0.112 → 0.1. While the Cloud offers additional multiple connector options and premium aluminum construction, the availability of functionally equivalent alternatives with superior measured specifications at significantly lower price impacts the cost-performance assessment.

## Reliability & Support

$$ \Large \text{0.6} $$

The Cloud provides standard reliability infrastructure with a 1-year warranty on the IEM and 3-month warranty on the cable. The detachable cable design improves practical longevity by addressing the most common failure mode in IEMs. Support is available through established dealers including Linsoul Audio and HiFiGo, offering email-based RMA procedures and standard defect coverage excluding damage from misuse. The precision CNC aluminum housing indicates robust mechanical construction, though no specific reliability data is available. BQEYZ's established market presence provides adequate but not exceptional reliability assurance within industry standards.
