## Overview

The Polk Audio TSi100 represents a 2-way passive bookshelf speaker from Polk's archived TSi series, featuring the company's proprietary Dynamic Balance technology developed through collaboration with Johns Hopkins University [1]. This speaker incorporates a 5.25" bi-laminate organic fiber cone woofer paired with a 1" silk dome tweeter, crossed over at 2,500 Hz using a 2nd order filter [2]. Built with extensively braced MDF construction and 3/4-inch baffles, the TSi100 was positioned as an affordable entry point to Polk's scientific driver optimization approach. The product is currently discontinued and only available through used markets, with dimensions of 11" H × 7" W × 10.25" D and weighing 10 pounds per speaker [1].

## Technology Level

$$ \Large \text{0.7} $$

The TSi100 demonstrates above-average technology level through proprietary Dynamic Balance driver optimization using laser interferometry research conducted jointly with Johns Hopkins University [3]. This technology employs laser imaging to identify and eliminate driver resonances, representing genuine scientific innovation beyond typical cost-reduction approaches. Additional technical features include Klippel Distortion Analyzer motor optimization, bi-laminate organic fiber cone construction, mylar bypass capacitors in crossover design, and extensively braced MDF cabinet construction [2][4]. The systematic approach to resonance elimination through material science represents meaningful technological advancement, though the purely analog/mechanical implementation limits potential compared to modern digital-integrated designs.

## Reliability & Support

$$ \Large \text{0.6} $$

The TSi100 offers above-standard warranty coverage with 5 years for both cabinet and drivers, exceeding typical 2-year industry standards [1]. Construction features solid MDF with extensive internal bracing designed to minimize resonances and provide inherent durability. Third-party reviews note the speakers may benefit from break-in period to achieve optimal sound characteristics and recommend adequate spacing for the rear-firing port design [6]. Polk Audio provides established customer service support through phone and email channels with warranty support for original retail purchasers. The discontinued/archived product status potentially limits long-term parts availability and future support duration, though current warranty claims remain honored through authorized dealers. Construction quality appears robust with proper cabinet bracing and component protection grilles included.

## Advice

For potential purchasers seeking affordable bookshelf speakers, consider newer alternatives providing equivalent functionality at lower cost. The Polk Audio T15 offers equivalent specifications and performance at 100 USD compared to the TSi100's 135 USD used market price, providing 26% cost savings with identical sensitivity and power handling. If specifically interested in Dynamic Balance technology, focus on current Polk products that incorporate updated implementations with better measured performance data. For used market purchases of the TSi100, ensure adequate spacing for the rear-firing port design and allow break-in period for optimal sound characteristics. Consider room size carefully, as the limited low-frequency extension may require subwoofer supplementation for larger listening environments.
