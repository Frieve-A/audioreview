## Overview

The Sony ECM-PCV80U is an electret condenser microphone designed specifically for PC recording, gaming, and content creation. Part of Sony's 60+ year heritage in professional audio engineering, this model features unidirectional pickup pattern and includes a dedicated USB Audio Box for digital connectivity. The microphone targets budget-conscious users seeking reliable PC recording capabilities with the backing of an established audio manufacturer.

## Scientific Validity

The ECM-PCV80U lacks critical measurement data required for comprehensive microphone evaluation. Available specifications show frequency response of 80-15,000 Hz, which falls short of the ideal 20Hz-20kHz range and represents limited bandwidth compared to modern microphone standards. Sensitivity is specified at -40dB ±3.5dB, but crucial performance metrics remain unavailable including S/N ratio (80dB+ transparent level), equivalent noise level (<10dB-A transparent level), and maximum SPL (140dB+ excellent level).
