## Overview

The Sony PlayStation Vita In-ear Headset PCH-ZHS1J was a dedicated gaming accessory designed specifically for Sony's PlayStation Vita handheld console. Released as part of the PS Vita ecosystem, this wired headset featured 10mm dynamic drivers with an integrated electret condenser microphone and basic inline controls. The product was manufactured by Sony Computer Entertainment and distributed globally as an official PS Vita accessory with regional variants including the PCH-ZHS1E model. Now discontinued, it represented Sony's approach to providing a complete audio solution for portable gaming, though with limited functionality beyond its intended gaming application.
