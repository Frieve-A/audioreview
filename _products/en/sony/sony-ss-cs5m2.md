## Overview

The SS-CS5M2 represents Sony's 2025 update to their popular CS series bookshelf speakers. This 3-way design incorporates a 5.12-inch Mica Reinforced Cellular (MRC) woofer, 1-inch dome tweeter, and dedicated 19mm super tweeter. The speakers achieve 53 Hz–50,000 Hz frequency response with crossover points at 2.5 kHz and 17 kHz. The product carries Hi-Res Audio certification, though this certification does not indicate scientifically demonstrated sound quality improvement. Updates from the previous SS-CS5 include revised tweeter design and crossover network for improved high-frequency detail. [1][2]

## Technology Level

$$ \Large \text{0.6} $$

The MRC woofer technology represents proprietary driver advancement over traditional paper cones, offering claimed rigidity improvements. ## Advice

Choose the SS-CS5M2 if you want a **3-way bookshelf speaker** at an exceptionally competitive 178 USD price point.
