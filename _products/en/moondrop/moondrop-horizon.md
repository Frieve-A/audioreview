## Overview

The MOONDROP HORIZON is a 50mm open-back over-ear headphone featuring the company's newly developed ULT (Ultra Low Distortion) dynamic driver technology. Priced at 159.99 USD, it incorporates a titanium-plated PEN dome with dual-layer liquid silica gel suspension composite diaphragm and FEA-optimized dual internal-external composite magnet circuit achieving 1.85T peak magnetic flux density. The headphone includes swappable 3.5mm and 4.4mm cable connectors and weighs approximately 350g. MOONDROP positions this as a professional-grade headphone utilizing their measurement-based tuning methodology with VDSF target curves.

## Technology Level

The HORIZON demonstrates significant technological advancement with proprietary ULT dynamic driver design incorporating titanium-plated PEN dome diaphragm and double-layer liquid silica gel suspension composite. The magnetic system utilizes two sets of N55 neodymium magnets with individual flux exceeding 1.6T, achieving 1.85T peak magnetic gap flux density through FEA-optimized dual internal-external composite circuit. Advanced materials engineering includes specialized diaphragm coating and suspension technologies representing contemporary driver development.
