## Overview

The Revel Concerta2 F35 is a 2.5-way floorstanding loudspeaker featuring three 5.25" aluminum cone woofers and a 1" aluminum tweeter with Acoustic Lens Waveguide technology. Derived from Revel's flagship Performa3 Series, the F35 represents the company's scientific approach to speaker design, incorporating decades of psychoacoustic research and measurement-based development. With crossover frequencies at 575Hz and 1.8kHz, the speaker demonstrates Revel's commitment to precision engineering and transparent audio reproduction. The F35 exemplifies Harman International's measurement-first philosophy, backed by extensive double-blind testing and anechoic chamber validation.

## Scientific Validity

$$ \Large \text{0.7} $$

The F35 demonstrates measurable performance achieving standard levels for speakers with some areas approaching transparent performance. Third-party measurements from Audio Science Review show frequency response of ±3.0 dB from 80 Hz to 20 kHz [1], which represents standard performance for speakers according to scientific criteria. Sensitivity measures 89.8 dB (2.83V @ 1M) [1], closely matching manufacturer specifications of 90 dB [2]. Low harmonic distortion is reported across midrange and midbass frequencies, with good horizontal directivity characteristics. Frequency response extension shows F3 at 61Hz (Erin's measurements) [2], while official specifications list 55Hz (-3dB) [3]. The speaker exhibits controlled directivity through the Acoustic Lens Waveguide technology. While transparent level requires ±1.0 dB deviation for excellent performance, the F35's ±3.0 dB places it solidly within standard performance range for speakers, approaching the transparent level threshold.
