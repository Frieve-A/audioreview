## Overview

The Shanling UA6 represents the flagship of Shanling's portable USB DAC/amplifier lineup, succeeding the UA5 with significant technical upgrades. Positioned at 159 USD, this USB dongle DAC features quad CS43131 DAC chip configuration, proprietary Hybrid Power Supply System 2.0 with 220mAh internal battery, and comprehensive connectivity including 3.5mm single-ended, 4.4mm balanced, and SPDIF digital outputs. Supporting high-resolution audio up to 768kHz/32-bit PCM and DSD512, the UA6 includes a 1.3-inch color display and gaming mode functionality in a compact aluminum housing.
