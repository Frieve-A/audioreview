## Overview

The Mason II Dignis Collaboration Model represents a limited edition partnership between Unique Melody and Korean leather brand Dignis, with only 200 units produced for the Japanese market. This universal fit IEM features 12 balanced armature drivers in a 3-way crossover configuration, platinum-plated metal sound tubes, and optimized airflow design. Driver configuration details vary slightly between sources: Mix Wave specifies "4 low, 4 mid, 4 high" while Head-Fi indicates "4 x Low, 4 x Mid, 2 x High, 2 x Super-high" [1][4]. The collaboration emphasizes premium aesthetics with a special Dignis leather case and travel-themed design philosophy targeting fatigue-free listening.
