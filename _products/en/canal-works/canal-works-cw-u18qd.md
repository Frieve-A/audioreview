## Overview

The Canal Works CW-U18QD is a universal in-ear monitor featuring a 2-way crossover design with 6 balanced armature drivers per ear [1]. Positioned at 622 USD in Canal Works' mid-to-upper range universal lineup [1], this model represents the Japanese manufacturer's approach to accessible multi-driver technology without custom molding requirements. Canal Works, established as one of Japan's few domestic custom IEM manufacturers [4], emphasizes traditional craftsmanship and professional monitoring applications [5]. The CW-U18QD targets users seeking multi-driver performance with universal fit convenience, competing in the premium IEM segment with aluminum nozzle construction and detachable 2-pin cable design [1].
