## Overview

The Canal Works CW-L18QD is a custom in-ear monitor featuring a 2-way crossover design with 6 balanced armature drivers, priced at approximately 899 USD [1]. Manufactured by Canal Works [2], a Japanese company based in Tokorozawa, Saitama Prefecture, the product is positioned in the premium custom IEM segment. The company specializes in professional-grade monitoring solutions for musicians, audio engineers, and audiophiles, operating since 2010 with a focus on custom-fit audio solutions. The CW-L18QD utilizes 4 drivers for high frequencies and 2 for low frequencies with detachable cables offering multiple connector options including 3.5mm standard, 2.5mm 4-pole balanced, and 4.4mm 5-pole balanced configurations [3]. Canal Works emphasizes Japanese craftsmanship and professional monitoring applications, though specific documentation for this particular model remains limited in publicly available sources.
