## Overview

The Fostex T60RPmk2ai represents the latest evolution in Fostex's RP planar magnetic headphone series, incorporating 4th generation driver technology with traditional Japanese craftsmanship. This semi-open design features a hard maple housing dyed with Awa indigo by traditional artisans, 7N grade high-purity OFC internal wiring, and Ultrasuede® earpads as standard equipment [1]. As the first Made in Japan model in the T60RP series, each unit is meticulously assembled at Fostex's domestic factory, combining 50+ years of RP driver development heritage with premium materials and construction.

## Technology Level

$$ \Large \text{0.8} $$

Fostex demonstrates substantial technical expertise with their proprietary 4th generation RP driver technology, representing continuous innovation since the original T50 debut in 1974 [2]. The redesigned printed coil pattern expands and uniformizes diaphragm vibration area, while increased magnets provide stronger controlled movement [2]. Optimized magnetic flux distribution suppresses unwanted diaphragm resonance, achieving improved sensitivity, smoother frequency characteristics, and enhanced transient response [2]. This patent-pending technology represents genuine advancement in planar magnetic design that competitors would find desirable to adopt. However, the purely analog/mechanical approach lacks integration of modern digital signal processing, software, or AI technologies that could provide additional performance benefits.
