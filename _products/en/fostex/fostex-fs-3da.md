## Overview

The Fostex FS-3DA is a 24bit/96kHz compatible USB DAC and headphone amplifier that was priced at approximately 151 USD at January 2016 exchange rates [1]. This discontinued product features stereo RCA line outputs, headphone output, optical S/PDIF input, and compact aluminum housing with wood-grain side panels. Current used market pricing averages approximately 130 USD based on available listings. Fostex designed the FS-3DA as a bus-powered solution requiring no external AC adapter, targeting users seeking a combination DAC and headphone amplifier for desktop audio systems.
