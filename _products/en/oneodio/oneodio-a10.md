## Overview

The OneOdio Focus A10 Hybrid Active Noise Cancelling Headphones represent the company's entry into the competitive budget ANC segment, with current pricing at 74.99 USD for Black variant (discounted from 89.99 USD) and 89.99 USD for Gray-Gold variant, featuring claimed 35dB noise reduction and extended battery life up to 62 hours. Featuring 40mm dynamic drivers with polymer diaphragms, dual-chamber acoustic design, and Hi-Res Audio certification, the Focus A10 targets consumers seeking affordable active noise cancellation without premium pricing. Available in Black and Gray-Gold variants, the headphones include hybrid ANC technology, Bluetooth 5.4 connectivity with multipoint pairing, quick charging capabilities, and comprehensive accessory package including EVA protective case and both wired and wireless connectivity options.

## Advice

For consumers seeking budget ANC headphones, the OneOdio Focus A10 at 74.99 USD represents competitive value, though consider the EarFun Wave Pro at 79.99 USD which provides enhanced LDAC codec support, longer battery life (80 hours ANC off, 55 hours ANC on), and comprehensive third-party validation. The Focus A10 appeals to users specifically seeking Hi-Res Audio certification and OneOdio's aesthetic design at a lower price point, while those prioritizing maximum battery life and codec support may prefer the Wave Pro despite the 5 USD premium. Potential buyers requiring reliable customer service should note both products have limited support infrastructure. The Focus A10 offers reasonable value at its price point for users comfortable with manufacturer specifications, while the Wave Pro provides additional verified performance data for users willing to spend slightly more.
