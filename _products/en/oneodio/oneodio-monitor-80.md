## Overview

OneOdio Monitor 80 represents the company's entry into professional monitoring headphones, featuring an open-back design with 40mm neodymium drivers [1]. Marketed for studio applications, the headphones include dual detachable cables and claim professional-grade audio reproduction [1]. At 99.99 USD, the Monitor 80 targets budget-conscious professionals and enthusiasts seeking monitoring capabilities without premium pricing [1].

## Advice

The OneOdio Monitor 80 represents the most affordable entry point into professional open-back monitoring headphones with extended frequency response specifications. While third-party measurement data remains limited, available verification from Soundphile Review supports basic manufacturer claims [3]. The 250Ω impedance requires adequate amplification for optimal performance, making it suitable for users with proper audio interfaces or headphone amplifiers. Given the 1.0 cost-performance score, no cheaper alternatives exist with equivalent specifications, making it the logical choice for budget-conscious professionals requiring these specific capabilities [1]. However, users requiring reliable warranty support should be aware of reported customer service difficulties and unusual warranty restrictions [2]. Those prioritizing comprehensive third-party validation should consider established alternatives like the Beyerdynamic DT 990 PRO, which offer superior measurement documentation at higher cost [4].
