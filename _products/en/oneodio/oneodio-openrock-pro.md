## Overview

The OneOdio OpenRock Pro represents the company's entry into the open-ear wireless earbuds market, targeting users seeking all-day battery life combined with situational awareness. Featuring 16.2mm dynamic drivers with proprietary TubeBass technology, these earbuds deliver 19 hours of single-charge playback and 46 hours total with the charging case. The open-ear design with adjustable silicon ear hooks aims to provide secure fit during sports activities while maintaining ambient sound awareness. At 109 USD, the OpenRock Pro competes in the mid-range segment of air conduction earbuds.

## Advice

The OneOdio OpenRock Pro offers exceptional battery life and open-ear design but faces cost-performance competition from the company's successor model, the OneOdio OpenRock S. The 19-hour single-charge performance significantly exceeds typical wireless earbuds, making it suitable for long workdays or extended activities without charging anxiety. Open-ear design provides essential situational awareness for runners, cyclists, and office workers. However, at 109 USD, the company's OpenRock S (99 USD) provides equivalent single-charge battery life, longer total battery life, and superior Bluetooth connectivity. Potential buyers should also note the absence of independent audio quality measurements, making sound performance difficult to verify objectively. Users seeking scientifically validated audio performance should consider alternatives with published third-party measurements.
