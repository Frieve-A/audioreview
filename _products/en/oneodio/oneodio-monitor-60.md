## Overview

The OneOdio Monitor 60 Professional Monitor Wired Headphones represent the company's attempt to deliver professional-grade monitoring equipment for audio professionals, audiophiles, and DJs. Featuring 50mm neodymium drivers in a closed-back over-ear design, the Monitor 60 includes Hi-Res Audio certification and extends its frequency response to 40kHz. The product includes multiple detachable cable options, swivel ear cups with 90-degree rotation capability, and targets studio monitoring, broadcasting, mixing, and personal listening applications. At 89.99 USD, it positions itself in the budget professional headphone market while offering features typically associated with higher-end monitoring equipment.

## Rationality of Design Philosophy

$$ \Large \text{0.1} $$

OneOdio's design philosophy prioritizes subjective appeal over scientific audio reproduction principles. The company explicitly states that "sound transcends mere technical specs—it's fundamentally emotional," demonstrating departure from measurement-driven design methodology essential for professional monitoring equipment. The deliberately implemented V-shaped tuning contradicts the fundamental purpose of monitor headphones: faithful reproduction of master sources. Company assessment indicates "rational response to market needs, but lacks sufficient scientific approach" in product development. The emphasis on entertainment value through bass and treble emphasis rather than neutral frequency response represents irrational design choices for professional monitoring applications, compromising the stated purpose of delivering accurate audio reproduction.

## Advice

The Monitor 60 may appeal to casual listeners seeking affordable headphones with professional aesthetics and Hi-Res Audio marketing appeal. However, audio professionals requiring accurate monitoring should consider alternatives with documented neutral frequency response and measurement-verified performance. The 24-month warranty provides value for users concerned about long-term reliability, but customer service quality issues warrant consideration. For budget-conscious purchasers, the Samson SR850 offers equivalent studio monitoring design with wider frequency response and professional reference tuning at significantly lower cost. Users requiring scientifically accurate monitoring should invest in products with published measurement data and neutral tuning characteristics rather than V-shaped entertainment-focused sound signatures.
