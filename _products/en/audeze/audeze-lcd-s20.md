## Overview

The Audeze LCD-S20 represents Audeze's first closed-back planar magnetic headphones, targeting professional audio environments with patent-pending SLAM (Symmetric Linear Acoustic Modulator) technology. Priced at 499 USD, the LCD-S20 features 90mm planar magnetic drivers with Audeze's proprietary technologies including Fluxor magnets, Fazor waveguides, and Ultra-Thin Uniforce diaphragms. The closed-back design aims to provide superior sound isolation for professional monitoring applications while delivering the low distortion characteristics associated with planar magnetic drivers.

## Technology Level

$$ \Large \text{0.8} $$

The LCD-S20 demonstrates significant technological advancement through multiple proprietary innovations. The patent-pending SLAM technology represents a breakthrough approach to controlling air pressure distribution within the earcup using precisely-tuned acoustic channels, enhancing low-end response below 100Hz. Audeze's established planar magnetic driver technology features 90mm drivers with Neodymium N50 magnets, Fluxor magnet arrays for optimized field distribution, Fazor phase management waveguides, and Ultra-Thin Uniforce diaphragm technology. This represents the first application of SLAM technology in the sub-500 USD price range, demonstrating technical expertise and innovation that other companies would find challenging to replicate quickly. The integration of multiple proprietary technologies shows advanced technical capability from an established planar magnetic technology leader.

## Cost-Performance

The H200 features THD <0.02% @ 100dB SPL (superior to LCD-S20's <0.1%), frequency response 2Hz-23.5kHz (wider low-end extension than LCD-S20's 10Hz-40kHz), passive isolation 40dB, sensitivity 97.5dB/1mW (higher than LCD-S20's 93dB/1mW), and includes the Headphone Utility plug-in. $$ \Large \text{0.8} $$

Warranty coverage includes 3 years for drivers and 1 year for other components from date of purchase, significantly exceeding the typical 2-year industry average.
