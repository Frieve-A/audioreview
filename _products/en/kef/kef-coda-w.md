## Overview

The KEF Coda W features 12th Generation Uni-Q coaxial drivers with 130mm magnesium/aluminum woofer and 25mm aluminum tweeter, delivering 100W Class D amplification per speaker (70W woofer + 30W tweeter) for 200W total system power. Priced at 999 USD per pair, connectivity includes HDMI ARC, Bluetooth 5.4 with aptX Lossless, USB-C 24bit/192kHz, and integrated MM phono preamp. Music Integrity Engine DSP provides crossover optimization and distortion reduction for mainstream listeners.
