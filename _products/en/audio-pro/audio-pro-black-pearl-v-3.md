## Overview

The Audio Pro Black Pearl V3 represents the Swedish manufacturer's traditional passive speaker approach before their transition to active wireless systems. Established in 1978, Audio Pro developed proprietary ACE-BASS technology to achieve enhanced bass performance from compact enclosures. This 2-way bass reflex bookshelf speaker features a 6.5-inch woofer and 0.75-inch soft dome tweeter with neodymium magnet and two-chamber aluminum case design. The product is now discontinued as Audio Pro has shifted focus entirely to powered speaker systems.
