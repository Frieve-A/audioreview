## Overview

The LETSHUOER S12 Ultra represents the culmination of the company's S12 series development, featuring a 14.8mm planar magnetic driver with refined internal construction [1]. Released in October 2025 as the "Finale of the S12 Legacy," it incorporates acoustic chamber optimization and modified damping based on user feedback from previous models. LETSHUOER, founded in 2016 by audio engineers with 16 years average experience, has established itself in the budget planar magnetic IEM segment. The S12 Ultra targets the competitive sub-200 USD market with aluminum alloy construction, interchangeable 3.5mm/4.4mm connectors, and a 392-core silver-plated copper cable [1]. Current market price is 169 USD through authorized channels including Amazon and Linsoul Audio [1][2].
