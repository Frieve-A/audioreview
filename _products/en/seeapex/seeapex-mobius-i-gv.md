## Overview

The SeeApex Mobius i GV represents SeeAudio's flagship ultra-premium offering, featuring a 14-driver hybrid configuration (1DD+9BA+4EST) housed in a Damascus steel shell with distinctive wave patterns [1]. Priced at 8,999 USD with global production limited to 66 pairs, this IEM positions itself around philosophical concepts of "imaginary numbers" and infinity while targeting the ultra-high-end audiophile market. Released in November 2025, the product extends SeeAudio's exploration of sound through advanced driver technology, premium materials, and artistic design elements that prioritize aesthetic and philosophical value alongside technical specifications.
