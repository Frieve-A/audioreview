## Overview

The MYER Audio SLIIVO SLA3 is a hybrid in-ear monitor featuring a single 10mm composite bio-diaphragm dynamic driver paired with three balanced armature drivers (two Knowles units plus one custom-developed driver). Manufactured by MYER Audio, described as a new and emerging brand in the HiFi audio industry, the SLA3 employs a three-way crossover system with the dynamic driver handling bass frequencies, dual Knowles BA drivers managing midrange, and a custom BA driver covering high frequencies. The earphones feature 3D-printed acoustic chambers and artistic face covers inspired by Aurora Borealis, with hand-assembly by professional engineers. Priced at 88.39 USD (sale price from 103.99 USD regular), the SLA3 includes a silver-plated copper cable with both 3.5mm and 4.4mm termination plugs included.
