## Overview

The MYER-AUDIO CKLVX CK2V represents a 7-driver hybrid in-ear monitor featuring a complex driver configuration combining 1x 10mm dynamic driver, 4x balanced armature drivers (including 2 Knowles units), and 2x micro-planar drivers. Manufactured by Shenzhen-based MYER AUDIO, a company founded in 2021 under Shenzhen Meier Technology Co. Ltd., the CK2V incorporates a 4-way frequency crossover with high-precision 3D-printed acoustic chambers. The product targets audiophiles seeking advanced multi-driver technology at competitive pricing, featuring interchangeable 3.5mm and 4.4mm termination plugs with detachable 0.78mm 2-pin cables.
