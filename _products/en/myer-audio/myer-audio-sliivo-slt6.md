## Overview

The MYER-AUDIO SLIIVO SLT6 is a four-unit balanced armature in-ear monitor featuring a sophisticated driver configuration with custom Sonion and Knowles units (1x Sonion composite unit, 2x Sonion standalone units, 1x Knowles composite unit). The earphones incorporate dual tuning switches offering four sound signatures, replaceable connector plugs for both 3.5mm and 4.4mm terminations, and a three-way crossover system. MYER-AUDIO claims over twenty years of industry experience and positions itself as a platform for high-quality innovation in the IEM market [1]. The product represents the company's approach to premium multi-driver balanced armature design, targeting audiophiles seeking customizable sound tuning options.
