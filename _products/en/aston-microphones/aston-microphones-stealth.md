## Overview

The Aston Stealth is an end-fire cardioid dynamic microphone featuring innovative hybrid technology that bridges traditional dynamic design with modern electronic enhancement. Measuring 196mm in length and 58mm in diameter with a weight of 695g, the Stealth incorporates a 25mm diaphragm within a distinctive aluminum and steel construction. The microphone's standout feature is its built-in Class A preamp that provides 50dB of gain boost when phantom power is detected, automatically switching between passive and active modes. Additional features include four selectable voicing modes (V1, V2, Guitar, Dark), removable stand mount with integrated pop filter, and purple LED indicators for active mode status.

## Technology Level

$$ \Large \text{0.7} $$

Aston demonstrates significant technological sophistication through proprietary design elements developed from ground-up engineering. The integration of Class A preamp circuitry with auto-detect phantom power functionality represents advanced electronic implementation within dynamic microphone design. Proprietary shock mounting system utilizes Sorbothane polymer suspension with 200g ballast optimization for capsule isolation. The four voicing modes employ contour networks rather than conventional filter circuitry, maintaining signal path integrity. Additional technical features include stainless steel Faraday cage for electromagnetic protection and fully balanced signal path architecture. The technology integration demonstrates competitive advantage duration requiring substantial development investment to replicate, positioning Aston ahead of conventional dynamic microphone approaches.

## Advice

The Aston Stealth represents excellent value for users requiring professional dynamic microphone performance with integrated preamp functionality. The 50dB gain boost eliminates need for external preamps in many applications, particularly valuable for home studios and portable recording setups. Four voicing modes provide genuine versatility for different source materials without requiring external processing. Users should ensure their audio interface provides adequate phantom power (48V) to utilize active mode capabilities. The microphone excels in applications where dynamic microphone characteristics are preferred but additional gain is essential. Consider this option when seeking to consolidate equipment while maintaining professional audio quality. The robust construction and extended warranty period support long-term professional use requirements.
