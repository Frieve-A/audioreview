## Overview

The final D7000 represents the company's flagship open-back planar magnetic headphones, incorporating their proprietary AFDS (Air Film Damping System) technology. Priced at 3,499 USD, these headphones feature aluminum magnesium alloy construction, custom-developed Pinna-Aligned Diffuser, and are designed with repairability in mind. final positions this product as a long-term investment piece, following their philosophy of creating audio products with enduring value like mechanical wristwatches.

## Technology Level

$$ \Large \text{0.9} $$

Exceptional technology integration featuring proprietary AFDS (Air Film Damping System) developed through collaboration with Sony microphone specialists. The system employs finite element method simulations and laser Doppler analysis to address diaphragm over-excursion in planar magnetic designs. Perforated metal screens positioned at precise distances create controlled air film damping, minimizing spurious resonances while preventing membrane-magnet contact. The newly developed Pinna-Aligned Diffuser and in-house manufacturing with custom production tools demonstrate high technical sophistication. The modular design enabling future upgrades represents forward-thinking engineering approach.

## Reliability & Support

$$ \Large \text{0.9} $$

Robust support infrastructure with global manufacturer warranty varying by region: 1 year standard, extending to 2 years in India and 3 years in UK. Simple planar magnetic structure with minimal moving parts inherently resists degradation. The modular design with screw-based assembly enables easy disassembly and repair. Post-warranty repair services available at reasonable costs: cable repair 20-30% of SRP, full replacement 30-50% of SRP. AFDS system provides inherent diaphragm protection against over-excursion damage. final's established track record in high-end audio products supports long-term reliability expectations.

## Advice

Consider the D7000 if you specifically value final's proprietary AFDS technology and premium construction quality, recognizing that no cheaper alternative provides equivalent functionality. The AFDS system offers theoretical advantages in diaphragm control, though measured performance validation is not publicly available. For buyers focused purely on basic planar magnetic performance without specialized features, standard planar options exist at significantly lower price points but lack the sophisticated air film damping and modular design. The unique technology integration and strong warranty support make this suitable for long-term investment if final's engineering philosophy resonates with your priorities. Ensure your amplification can properly drive 50Ω impedance at 89dB/mW sensitivity for optimal performance.
