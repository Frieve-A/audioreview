## Overview

The beyerdynamic DT 1770 PRO MKII represents the German manufacturer's flagship closed-back studio headphones, featuring the newly developed TESLA.45 driver technology. Released in 2025, these professional monitoring headphones target recording, monitoring, and editing applications with a 30Ω impedance design for wide device compatibility. The headphones incorporate beyerdynamic's signature TESLA technology with magnetic flux density exceeding one Tesla, manufactured at their Heilbronn facility with precision quality control. Key features include two ear pad sets (velour and artificial leather), dual cable configuration (3m straight, 5m coiled) with XLR connectors, and comprehensive accessory package including hard case.

## Technology Level

$$ \Large \text{0.9} $$

The TESLA.45 driver technology demonstrates exceptional engineering sophistication with proprietary design elements that competitors would find challenging to replicate. Key technological achievements include magnetic flux density exceeding one Tesla through optimized neodymium magnet systems, PEEK polymer diaphragm construction, and aluminum-coated copper voice coil design [3]. The driver development represents recent technological advancement specifically targeting reduced total harmonic distortion and improved impulse behavior compared to predecessor models. Manufacturing occurs in-house at beyerdynamic's German facility with multiple measurement stages during production ensuring strict quality tolerances [4]. The technology integration demonstrates significant technical expertise accumulation and proprietary patent applications, though it maintains primarily analog/mechanical approach without advanced digital integration.
