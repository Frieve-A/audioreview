## Overview

The TRN BA16 represents TRN Audio's flagship entry in the premium in-ear monitor market, featuring an ambitious 16 balanced armature drivers per side configuration. Released in September 2023 at 234 USD, the BA16 distinguishes itself through electronic three-way crossover design and six selectable sound signatures via three adjustable tuning switches. The driver allocation spans frequency ranges with 6 drivers for high frequencies, 6 for midrange, 3 for mid-low, and 1 for low frequency per side. TRN's positioning targets enthusiasts seeking customizable tuning options within the balanced armature flagship segment, competing against established manufacturers through aggressive driver count and modularity features including interchangeable cable terminations supporting 2.5mm, 3.5mm, and 4.4mm connections.
