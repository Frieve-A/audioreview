## Overview

The TRN ORCA is a budget-oriented in-ear monitor featuring a 10mm dual-magnet dynamic driver with customizable tuning capabilities [1][2]. Priced at 15.99 USD for the standard version [2], it incorporates contemporary driver technology including a high-polymer crystal LCP diaphragm, N52 magnetic circuit, and copper-clad aluminum voice coil within a liquid metal cavity design [1][2]. The distinguishing feature is a three-stage tuning switch system providing six customizable sound modes [1][2], targeting users seeking tonal flexibility in the budget segment. TRN, established in 2017, positions this product as an entry-level offering combining functional versatility with aggressive pricing.
