## Overview

Cear Corporation's Pave CP-4000 represents an innovative approach to portable speaker design, incorporating proprietary spatial audio processing technology within a compact cube-shaped form factor. Founded in 2018 and based in Tokyo, Cear has developed specialized algorithms for sound field control and multi-speaker coordination. The CP-4000 features advanced connectivity including aptX Lossless and LE Audio Auracast support, dual 15W drivers totaling 30W output, and IPX5-6 water resistance. The company's mission to "harness the power of sound and cutting-edge technology to create new values and experiences" is evident in their implementation of real-time audio analysis and crosstalk cancellation technologies.
