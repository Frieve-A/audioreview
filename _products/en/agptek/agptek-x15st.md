## Overview

The AGPTEK X15ST is a budget-oriented digital audio player featuring 16GB internal storage, Bluetooth 4.0 connectivity, and a 1.8-inch TFT color touch screen. Manufactured by AGPTEK, a company established in 2008 specializing in portable audio devices, this player targets users seeking basic music playback functionality at an affordable price point. The device supports multiple audio formats including MP3, WMA, APE, FLAC, and WAV, with additional features such as FM radio, voice recording, and e-book reading capabilities.
