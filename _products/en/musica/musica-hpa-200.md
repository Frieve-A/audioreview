## Overview

The Musica HPA-200 is a stereo headphone amplifier manufactured by Musica, a small independent Japanese Hi-Fi company founded in 2005. This semiconductor-based amplifier features dual headphone outputs, single line input/output, and gold-plated connectors. The company primarily focuses on pre-amplifiers, power amplifiers, graphic equalizers, and super tweeters, with the HPA-200 being available through select dealers rather than prominently featured in their main product catalog. At approximately 1,336 USD (135,000 rubles), the HPA-200 targets discerning audiophiles seeking dedicated headphone amplification with claimed "100% sound quality."
