## Overview

The DARUMA AUDIO X-Star is a planar magnetic in-ear monitor developed by the China-based company founded in 2019. Priced at 264 USD, the X-Star features a precision-machined aluminum body housing planar magnetic drivers and includes a comprehensive accessory package with an OCC silver-plated cable supporting both 3.5mm and 4.4mm terminations. The product distinguishes itself through an interchangeable nozzle filter system offering three different acoustic tuning options (blue, silver, gold), allowing users to customize the sound signature according to preference. Key specifications include 16Ω impedance, 100dB sensitivity, 20Hz-20kHz frequency response, and 5.3g weight per earpiece. The X-Star was developed with the concept of achieving "natural, live-like immersive experience" and ships with multiple ear tip sizes, a carrying pouch, and user manual.
