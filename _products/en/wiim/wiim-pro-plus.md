## Overview

The WiiM Pro Plus is an enhanced network music streamer announced by Linkplay Technology Inc. in August 2023. It builds upon the popular WiiM Pro with three main enhancements: a premium AKM 4493SEQ Digital-to-Analog Converter supporting up to 768 kHz PCM and DSD512, a TI Burr-Brown PCM1861 Analog-to-Digital Converter enabling line-in signals from turntables to be networked, and enhanced clock/power/circuit design reducing audio processing noise. The device serves as an affordable audiophile-grade streaming solution that transforms existing analog audio equipment into smart, network-connected systems while maintaining budget-friendly pricing at 219 USD.

## Scientific Validity

$$ \Large \text{0.9} $$

Third-party measurements from Audio Science Review [1] and HiFi Studio 79 [2] demonstrate exceptional performance across all measured parameters. SINAD measured at 115.2 dB (ASR production sample) indicates excellent signal purity, significantly exceeding professional requirements. THD of 0.00022% represents outstanding distortion control well within audible thresholds. Dynamic range of 119.3 dB provides excellent noise floor performance. Crosstalk of -119.2 dB demonstrates superior channel separation. Frequency response measurements confirm flat performance across the tested bandwidth. All measured metrics achieve excellent performance levels with comprehensive third-party verification from multiple independent sources confirming a significant 24 dB SINAD improvement over the original WiiM Pro.

## Technology Level

$$ \Large \text{0.6} $$

The WiiM Pro Plus represents competent engineering with appropriate contemporary technology implementation. Linkplay demonstrates good engineering integration with in-house design and accumulated know-how from experienced team members with backgrounds from Google, Broadcom, InterVideo, and Harman. The product incorporates established licensed audio components including the AKM 4493SEQ DAC introduced in 2018-2022 and TI Burr-Brown PCM1861 ADC using mature technology. No other network streamer at equivalent price achieves comparable measured performance with similar components, indicating implementation expertise in integrating DAC, ADC, WiFi streaming, and DSP within a single device. The advanced integration of computers, cloud services, and digital signal processing qualifies for positive evaluation. However, the implementation relies on standard licensed components with no proprietary patents or unique audio technologies, and the core DAC/ADC chips are mature and widely available.

## Reliability & Support

$$ \Large \text{0.6} $$

The WiiM Pro Plus offers standard 1-year manufacturer warranty with global support system provided directly by WiiM through their mobile app and email channels. The company maintains 24-hour response targets and provides regular firmware updates with approximately monthly releases throughout 2024-2025. Latest firmware version 4.8.805453 was released January 23, 2026. Known issues include isolated reports of power-related failures after extended unplugging, Ethernet port malfunctions, 12V trigger functionality problems, and intermittent Wi-Fi connectivity issues. However, these represent anecdotal forum reports without statistical failure data. The moderate design complexity of a digital streamer with multiple connectivity options provides reasonable inherent reliability, though the 1-year warranty period is below industry average.

## Rationality of Design Philosophy

$$ \Large \text{0.8} $$

The WiiM Pro Plus demonstrates a rational design philosophy with measurement-focused scientific approach and complete elimination of subjective approaches. Costs are allocated directly to performance-improving components including the professional-grade AKM 4493SEQ DAC and quad-core A53 CPU rather than premium materials or aesthetic enhancements. The model represents significant functional progression over its predecessor with documented 24 dB SINAD improvement while maintaining cost-effectiveness under 250 USD. Linkplay achieves high measured performance through effective cost reduction strategies, mass production efficiency, and modern digital signal processing implementation including DSP-based room correction. The approach prioritizes objective performance metrics over traditional audiophile approaches, demonstrating innovative cost optimization and rational technology adoption for meaningful user benefits.

## Advice

The WiiM Pro Plus is recommended for users seeking to add high-quality network streaming and DAC functionality to existing audio systems without significant investment. Its exceptional measured performance rivals products costing significantly more while providing comprehensive connectivity and streaming protocol support. The device is ideal for users with analog sources requiring digitization, those wanting room correction capabilities, and anyone seeking to integrate legacy equipment into modern streaming ecosystems. Users requiring premium build quality, extended warranty coverage, or on-site support services should consider higher-priced alternatives, though none currently offer equivalent measured performance at this price point.
