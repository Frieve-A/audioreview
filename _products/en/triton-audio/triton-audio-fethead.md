## Overview

The Triton Audio FetHead is an in-line microphone preamplifier designed to provide 27dB of clean gain for dynamic and ribbon microphones. This Class A JFET buffer operates on 48V phantom power and connects directly between microphone and audio interface via balanced XLR connections. Manufactured by Dutch boutique company Triton Audio, the FetHead targets recording applications where additional clean gain is needed ahead of the main preamp. The device features a compact metal barrel construction and blocks phantom power from reaching connected microphones, making it safe for ribbon microphones that could be damaged by direct phantom power.
