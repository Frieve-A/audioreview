## Overview

The Nothing Ear (3) represents the company's latest iteration in true wireless earbuds, featuring 12mm dynamic drivers with PMI + TPU diaphragm construction and comprehensive codec support including LDAC for high-resolution audio. The earbuds incorporate real-time adaptive ANC with claimed 45dB noise reduction, Bluetooth 5.4 connectivity, and a comprehensive microphone system with 3 MEMS mics plus 1 VPU per side. Battery performance offers 5.5 hours with ANC enabled, extending to 10 hours with ANC disabled, totaling over 40 hours with the charging case. The product targets users seeking modern wireless features with high-resolution audio capabilities and active noise cancellation [2].
