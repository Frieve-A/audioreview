## Overview

The Lafitear LV is a hybrid in-ear monitor featuring a combination of 1 dynamic driver and 1 planar driver configuration. Positioned in the budget IEM segment at 26 USD, the product targets consumers seeking multi-driver technology at accessible pricing. The earphones feature detachable 2-pin 0.78mm cables with options for single crystal copper silvered or 6N monocrystalline copper silver plating materials. Available with or without microphone options, the LV represents Lafitear's entry into the competitive hybrid driver market segment.
