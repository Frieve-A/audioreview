## Overview

The Lafitear LM1 is a budget hybrid in-ear monitor that combines a 10mm titanium crystal diaphragm dynamic driver with a 6.8mm electret electrostatic driver. Priced at approximately 15 USD on AliExpress, this Chinese IEM features hybrid driver technology with dual-driver configuration. The LM1 features a PC cavity imported from Germany, ergonomic design with 45° sound outlet, and detachable 0.78mm 2-pin cable. With specifications including 20Hz-20kHz frequency response, 20Ω impedance, and 115dB sensitivity, the LM1 implements hybrid driver technology for budget-conscious consumers seeking dual-driver configuration.
