## Overview

The Lafitear LF4 represents a budget-tier hybrid in-ear monitor featuring a 1DD+3BA driver configuration developed by Foshan Shunde Lafiaudio Electronics Co., Ltd., a Chinese manufacturer founded in 2017. The company specializes in Bluetooth earbuds, wired earphones, and headphones, positioning the LF4 as an entry-level option targeting cost-conscious consumers seeking hybrid driver technology. The product employs traditional 1 dynamic driver + 3 balanced armature configuration per earpiece with detachable cable system and metal-plastic construction, marketed with "HIFI Heavy Bass" sound signature claims and total "8 drivers" marketing terminology referring to the combined driver count across both earpieces.
