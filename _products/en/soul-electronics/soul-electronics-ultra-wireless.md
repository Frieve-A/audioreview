## Overview

Soul Electronics Ultra Wireless represents a basic entry in the wireless over-ear headphone market, released in 2019 by the company founded in 2010 as "SOUL by Ludacris." The headphones feature conventional 40mm neodymium drivers with Bluetooth 5.0 connectivity, targeting users seeking extended battery life with 36 hours of playback time. Despite the brand's stated focus on "technology, innovation, and creating wireless audio products that emphasize power, clarity, and comfort," the Ultra Wireless demonstrates a conservative approach to design and feature implementation. The product launched at approximately 60 USD and competes in a crowded market segment with numerous alternatives offering superior specifications and features.

## Technology Level

$$ \Large \text{0.3} $$

The Ultra Wireless employs mature, widely-available technologies without notable innovation or technical advancement. Bluetooth 5.0 connectivity represents appropriate contemporary technology for its 2019 release date, while the 40mm neodymium drivers utilize standard transducer technology with no proprietary developments. The implementation lacks modern features such as active noise cancellation, advanced codec support beyond basic profiles (AD2P/HFP/AVRCP), or digital signal processing capabilities. No patent technology adoption is evident, and the design demonstrates basic analog/wireless integration without sophisticated engineering approaches. The 320mAh battery and charging system represent conventional implementation without efficiency innovations. Compared to contemporaneous products incorporating active noise cancellation, adaptive EQ, or multipoint connectivity, the Ultra Wireless reflects a conservative approach that prioritizes simplicity over technological advancement.

## Reliability & Support

$$ \Large \text{0.4} $$

Soul Electronics provides standard warranty coverage with 1-year limited warranty for US customers and 2-year coverage for European Union customers, representing average industry practice. The company offers email-based customer support with structured case number and RMA systems [4]. ## Advice

The Soul Electronics Ultra Wireless targets users prioritizing extended battery life and basic wireless functionality over audio performance and modern features. Consider this product only if your primary requirement is long playback duration (36 hours) and you exclusively use headphones in quiet environments where poor passive isolation is not problematic. However, the significant cost-performance disadvantage compared to alternatives like the KVIDIO headphones (offering superior battery life at 38% of the price) makes the Ultra Wireless difficult to recommend for most users. For similar or lower cost, alternatives provide active noise cancellation, better isolation, or superior specifications. If you require over-ear headphones with extended battery life, research current models from established manufacturers that incorporate active noise cancellation and modern codec support, as these features have become standard expectations in this price range. The documented durability concerns with headband connection points suggest careful handling requirements that may limit practical longevity.
