## Overview

The KZ Pisces represents the company's approach to affordable dual dynamic driver in-ear monitors, featuring a horizontal dual-driver stacking structure with 7mm and 8mm dynamic drivers positioned side-by-side. Founded in 2008 in Dongguan, China, KZ Audio ("Knowledge Zenith") has established itself as a prominent Chi-Fi brand focused on delivering multi-driver configurations at competitive price points. The Pisces offers frequency response from 20-40,000 Hz, 18Ω impedance (adjustable to 18-22Ω with tuning switches), and 102 dB/mW sensitivity. Available in standard and advanced editions, the latter includes 4-level tuning switches for frequency response adjustment. The detachable 0.78mm 2-pin cable design with silver-plated OFC construction and claimed noise isolation up to 36 dB positions this as an entry-level option for users seeking dual-driver technology without premium pricing.

## Technology Level

$$ \Large \text{0.6} $$

The horizontal dual-driver stacking implementation demonstrates legitimate engineering innovation. The 7mm and 8mm dynamic drivers positioned side-by-side represent a departure from conventional single-driver designs, with claimed acoustic benefits through shortened low-frequency transmission paths and optimized mid/high frequency routing. Professional-grade filters enable frequency band adjustment, while the 4-level tuning switch system in advanced versions provides measurable frequency response modification capabilities. Detachable 0.78mm 2-pin gold-plated connectors and silver-plated OFC cable technology indicate attention to signal transmission considerations. However, the implementation remains primarily analog/mechanical without advanced digital integration or cutting-edge driver technologies found in contemporary high-performance designs. The technology demonstrates competency within KZ's established expertise in multi-driver configurations, though the competitive advantage duration appears limited as similar approaches become increasingly accessible to other manufacturers.
