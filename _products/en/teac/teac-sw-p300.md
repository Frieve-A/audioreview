## Overview

The TEAC SW-P300 is a compact active subwoofer released in mid-2015, featuring a 200mm cone driver powered by an ICEpower 50ASX2-SE Class D amplifier delivering 91W maximum output [1][2]. The product includes adjustable crossover frequency (40Hz-200Hz), phase switching, and both line and speaker-level inputs with through output capability. Originally positioned as an affordable desktop subwoofer solution, the product has been discontinued [1] and is currently available only in limited quantities through third-party sellers.
