## Overview

The Mercedes-Benz A2238209903 Bluetooth® headphones represent the luxury automotive brand's entry into personal audio equipment. These over-ear headphones feature hybrid active noise cancelling, Bluetooth 5.0 connectivity, and an 18-hour battery life with ANC disabled. Positioned as a premium accessory, they include luxury brand design elements and are marketed through Mercedes-Benz's official accessory channels. The headphones support both wireless and wired connections via a 3.5mm jack, with touchpad controls on the right earpiece for intuitive operation.
