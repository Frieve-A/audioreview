The design combines 54mm neodymium drivers in an open-back configuration with premium materials including genuine leather headband and memory foam ear pads. Originally targeting both consumer and professional audio applications, the AQUSTA sits within TELEFUNKEN's consumer headphone lineup as a vintage-inspired product emphasizing traditional craftsmanship over technological innovation.
