## Overview

ELPA ASAHI ELECTRIC CO., LTD (ASAHI ELECTRIC CO., LTD) is a Japanese consumer electronics manufacturer founded in March 1970 and headquartered in Daito City, Osaka. With annual sales of 20.185 billion yen and global presence across Asia, the company operates under the vision of "People-to-People Mutual Trust" and commitment to contributing to community through products for better life. The company specializes in diverse electronics categories including AVC products (headphones, earphones, Bluetooth speakers), lighting, electrical wiring, telecommunications, security equipment, and household appliances. Their audio product range includes radio earphones, TV earphones, canal type earphones, wireless stereo headphones, and compact Bluetooth speakers with NFC connectivity. ELPA focuses on practical consumer electronics with emphasis on affordability and essential functionality rather than high-performance audio segments.
