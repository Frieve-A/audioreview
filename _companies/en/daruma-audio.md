## Overview

DARUMA AUDIO, formally registered as NTS Inc. and founded in 2013, is a Tokyo-based audio equipment manufacturer specializing in high-end in-ear monitors. The company has developed a focused product portfolio including flagship models like the T-800 Vento Conductor, CELESTIAL, and Suzuran RAN. DARUMA AUDIO distinguishes itself through proprietary acoustic chamber technology and strategic manufacturing partnerships for cost optimization. The company operates through direct online sales with comprehensive customer support infrastructure and regularly introduces collaboration products and limited editions targeting audio enthusiasts.
