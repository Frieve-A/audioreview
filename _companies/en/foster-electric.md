## Overview

Foster Electric Company, Limited, founded in 1949, is a major Japanese OEM manufacturer specializing in speakers and acoustic components. The company operates the Fostex brand for professional and consumer audio products, with headquarters in Akishima City, Tokyo. As one of the world's largest OEM manufacturers, Foster Electric produces acoustic components for automotive applications while Fostex (established 1973) focuses on professional monitors, headphones, and high-fidelity audio equipment. Key product categories include professional monitor speakers (6301 series, PM series), planar magnetic headphones (RP technology), automotive acoustic components, and OEM speaker manufacturing. The company's legacy includes the 6301 series monitors, which became industry standards in broadcasting facilities since 1982, and pioneering Regular Phase (RP) planar magnetic technology first developed in 1974.
