## Overview

VIE Style is a neurotechnology company specializing in brain-science-based audio products that combine traditional headphone functionality with electroencephalogram (EEG) monitoring capabilities. Founded with a mission to optimize mental states through music and brain stimulation, the company has developed innovative "in-ear EEG" technology that enables wireless brainwave detection during daily listening activities. Their product portfolio includes the VIE Shair headphones with patented 3D air frame design, VIE EEG headphones for research-grade brain monitoring, and VIE FIT true wireless earphones. The company operates research division VIE Lab and collaborates with major telecommunications companies like KDDI on advanced neuro-tech applications.

## Cost-Performance

$$ \Large \text{0.6} $$

This site evaluates based solely on functionality and measured performance values, without considering driver types or configurations.

Cost-performance evaluation reveals mixed results across VIE Style's product portfolio. The VIE Shair at 254 USD faces significant competition from more affordable alternatives like the Fostex T50RP MK3 at 145 USD. The Fostex provides equivalent audio functionality with comparable frequency response characteristics, with third-party measurements showing 15Hz-35kHz frequency response range and ±3-5dB deviations. CP = 145 USD ÷ 254 USD = 0.57, rounded to 0.6. While the Fostex requires wired connection compared to VIE Shair's Bluetooth capability, this functional difference can be addressed through addition of a Bluetooth receiver accessory for approximately 25 USD, maintaining cost advantage. The HiFiMan Deva at 299 USD offers superior wireless functionality with LDAC and aptX-HD codec support compared to VIE Shair's Bluetooth 4.2, but is priced higher. The VIE EEG headphone cannot be evaluated due to lack of comparable products combining research-grade EEG with audio functionality. The VIE FIT2 appears significantly overpriced compared to alternatives like JLab Go Pods ANC at 36 USD, which offers equivalent audio performance with IPX5 rating and active noise cancellation functionality, though it lacks the neurotechnology ecosystem integration that VIE products provide.

## Advice

VIE Style products suit users seeking cutting-edge neurotechnology integration with audio equipment. The VIE EEG headphone is recommended for researchers, universities, and professionals requiring portable EEG monitoring with integrated audio capabilities, offering research-grade accuracy at significantly lower cost than traditional laboratory equipment. The VIE Shair appeals to audiophiles interested in innovative comfort design with 3D air frame technology, though potential buyers should verify audio performance through third-party measurements before purchase due to limited available data.
