## Overview

Auraphon Audio is a new audio company founded on December 4, 2023, with the mission of "making the world a better sounding place." The company focuses on producing high-quality audio products at reasonable prices for a broad target audience including musicians, DJs, music lovers, streamers, video makers, content creators, gamers, and movie fans. The company emphasizes cost-efficient production approaches. The company's flagship product is the PS3 powered bookshelf speaker, developed in collaboration with German company reProducer, inheriting their sound design while targeting a broader consumer market.

## Cost-Performance

$$ \Large \text{1.0} $$

At approximately 317 USD (based on Japanese pricing of ¥49,500 [4] converted at current exchange rates), comprehensive market research confirms that the PS3 is the world's cheapest product with equivalent-or-better functions and performance. The JBL 305P MkII at 318 USD per pair [6] provides equivalent-or-better output (82W total vs PS3's 60W total) and frequency response (49Hz-20kHz vs PS3's 68Hz-22kHz), but lacks Bluetooth 5.4 and USB-C DAC. When bridging these functional gaps with minimal accessories (Bluetooth adapter approximately 10 USD, USB-C DAC approximately 20 USD), the total cost reaches approximately 348 USD, exceeding the PS3's 317 USD. Since no equivalent-or-better product exists at a lower price than the PS3, CP = 1.0.

## Reliability & Support

$$ \Large \text{0.3} $$

Reliability and support present significant concerns due to the company's recent establishment. Founded less than two years ago, Auraphon Audio lacks an established reliability track record or long-term support commitment verification. The support infrastructure appears limited to email contact only, with no evidence of comprehensive global support networks. While the PS3 features sturdy metal construction with spike-based insulator feet using reProducer Epic series components, indicating reasonable build quality, the absence of warranty information and unknown repair systems create uncertainty. The company's manufacturing approach emphasizes cost efficiency, but actual failure rate data is unavailable due to the product's recent market introduction.

## Advice

Auraphon Audio represents a new entry in the affordable powered speaker market with some compelling features but significant limitations. While the PS3 offers modern connectivity including Bluetooth 5.4, VU meters, and comprehensive input options, its cost-performance is limited by established alternatives like the JBL 305P MkII that provide equivalent-or-better output and performance at similar price points. The company's limited track record, support infrastructure concerns, and lack of comprehensive measurement data create additional uncertainty. Potential buyers seeking established reliability, proven performance, or better value should consider products from more established manufacturers with published third-party measurements and comprehensive support networks.
