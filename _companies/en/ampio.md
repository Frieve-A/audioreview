## Overview

AMPIO is a Taiwanese audio company launched as a brand in November 2017 by Vencer Co., Ltd. (established 2007), with headquarters in New Taipei City, Taiwan. The company specializes in portable audio solutions, focusing primarily on Bluetooth DAC/amplifiers for consumer markets. AMPIO's product lineup centers around portable amplifiers, including models like the VS-1880 and VD-6880 designed for smartphone and mobile device connectivity. The company employs established audio component architectures using proven chipsets from major suppliers, positioning products in budget-conscious market segments while maintaining compatibility with high-quality audio codecs including LDAC and aptX protocols. Note: Primary product VS-1880 was launched in 2017 with limited current market availability.
