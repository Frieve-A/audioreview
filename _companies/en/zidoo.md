## Overview

Shenzhen Zidoo Technology Co., Ltd., founded in 2014, operates two product lines: 4K/8K HDR media players under the Zidoo brand, and high-fidelity network streamers, DAC, and amplifier products under the wholly-owned Eversolo sub-brand. The company employs more than 60 R&D engineers and exports over 80% of its products to Europe, North America, and Southeast Asia [1]. Eversolo products have received industry recognition in international audio award programs.

## Scientific Validity

$$ \Large \text{0.8} $$

Third-party measurements from independent laboratories cover representative products across both brands.

**Zidoo NEO S** — SoundStage Network measurements using an Audio Precision APx555 B Series analyzer (balanced XLR output, 200kΩ load) [2]: THD <0.00025% at 24/96, THD+N <0.00036%, S/N ratio 121.6 dB (16/44.1), dynamic range 124.8–126.0 dB (24/96), crosstalk -121.2 dB at 1kHz, frequency response deviation ±0.03 dB (20Hz–20kHz), IMD SMPTE <-97 dB (24/96). All metrics demonstrate excellent measured performance across every reported parameter.

**Eversolo AMP-F10** — Stereophile measurements [3]: THD+N 0.008% at 1kHz, SNR >113 dB A-weighted, crosstalk >-95 dB at 1kHz, damping factor >600 at 1kHz, frequency response ±0.5 dB (10Hz–30kHz). All metrics demonstrate excellent amplifier performance.

Across both measured products, all reported metrics achieve excellent levels. The portfolio demonstrates consistently strong audio engineering across the DAC/streamer and power amplifier categories.

## Technology Level

$$ \Large \text{0.5} $$

Zidoo's product engineering is built entirely on commercially available third-party components: Realtek RTD1619DR and Amlogic S928X-K SoCs, ESS Technology DAC chips (ES9038Pro, ES9068, ES9069Q), XMOS USB audio receivers, and Accusilicon femtosecond clocks. No independently verified proprietary patents in audio or video processing have been identified. The "Dual HDMI Audio-Video Separation" claim carries no patent documentation and has not been verified by independent parties. ZDMC (Zidoo Media Center) is a fork of open-source Kodi, and the VS10 video engine is Dolby's licensed technology integrated via the Realtek SoC, not Zidoo's own development.

Zidoo maintains an in-house R&D team of 60+ engineers responsible for hardware design, Android OS customization, and proprietary software, representing genuine design ownership rather than OEM procurement. The Poster Wall media management software (version 4.0) reflects accumulated expertise built over more than a decade of Android media player development. However, this software differentiation provides approximately a 1–2 year competitive lead, not the 3+ years that would indicate a durable moat. Technology integration — combining digital processing, audio circuitry, and Android software — is appropriate for the product category but not differentiated relative to industry peers. DSD512 specifications, MQA hardware decoding, and femtosecond clock promotion are predominantly marketing-oriented feature additions without substantiated performance differentiation. These factors balance the genuine design ownership advantage, yielding a net neutral adjustment.

## Reliability & Support

$$ \Large \text{0.4} $$

The manufacturer warranty is 1 year for all products, below the 2-year industry average. Active OTA firmware updates are provided for current-generation products; firmware v1.3.05 was released January 2026 addressing multiple playback issues across the 2024 8K product line. However, community reports across multiple forums, along with the Futeko UK support community becoming read-only in October 2025, document a consistent pattern of reduced firmware support for older product lines after newer lines launch. Effective support duration is assessed as shorter than typical. Repair and after-sales service is centralized in Shenzhen with no documented regional service centers outside China. Launch-state firmware instability has been reported across multiple product generations, with most critical issues resolved through subsequent updates. No statistical failure rate data or MTBF figures are publicly available.

## Rationality of Design Philosophy

$$ \Large \text{0.6} $$

Zidoo's core engineering choices are scientifically rational: solid-state designs throughout, ESS Technology DAC chips with well-characterized low-distortion and high dynamic range performance, a dual power supply topology separating analog and digital rails to minimize interference, and Roon Ready certification requiring third-party interoperability validation. The cinephile+audiophile integration concept — combining 8K HDR video playback with audiophile-grade balanced DAC outputs in a single enclosure — represents a well-defined product rationale that justifies dedicated-equipment existence for its target audience. This integration is a genuinely differentiated approach in the market.

Performance progression across generations is clearly documented: video capability has advanced from 4K (2016) through 4K HDR to current 8K HDR; DAC implementation has progressed from ES9038Pro through ES9068 to ES9069Q with measurably improving specifications; streaming protocol support (Roon Ready, TIDAL Connect, AirPlay 2) has expanded across generations; and the Eversolo sub-brand was launched to serve audio-focused users with a dedicated product line. These represent directed, performance-oriented evolution.

The philosophy is partially undermined in three areas. DSD512 is marketed as superior to PCM without scientific substantiation. MQA hardware decoding is promoted as delivering improved audio quality despite documented encoding artifacts and the absence of demonstrated audible benefit. Femtosecond clock specifications are cited as audible improvements at levels where audibility has not been established. These constitute claims of audible effects for scientifically unsubstantiated or contested elements, applying a downward adjustment that partially offsets the positive factors above.

## Advice

Zidoo and Eversolo products deliver verified excellent measured performance across DAC, streamer, and power amplifier categories. For pure audio streaming use cases, the Eversolo lineup — including the DMP-A6 Gen 2 — offers competitive price-to-performance ratios at multiple price points. The Zidoo NEO S is notable as the lowest-priced product identified combining third-party-verified high SNR, balanced XLR line output, and balanced 4.4mm headphone amplification in a single unit.

Prospective buyers should account for the 1-year warranty and the documented pattern of firmware support reductions for older product lines. For buyers who prioritize extended firmware support or longer warranty coverage, alternatives from manufacturers with more generous warranty terms are worth comparing. For buyers primarily focused on verified measured audio performance at competitive prices, particularly within the Eversolo lineup, the measured performance data is compelling. Users seeking integrated 8K media playback alongside audiophile-grade balanced DAC output in a single device will find this combination difficult to match at similar price points from separate components.
