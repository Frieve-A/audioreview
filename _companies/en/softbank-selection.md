## Overview

SoftBank SELECTION operates as an Internet-first brand for multi-category smartphone accessories, founded in 2008. The company markets audio products under the GLIDiC brand, which operates under the slogan "Sound for people on the move," positioning itself as a mobile-oriented audio specialist. Their flagship GLIDiC TW-9100 True Wireless Earbuds represent the culmination of their audio expertise, featuring hybrid Knowles balanced armature and dynamic driver configurations with adaptive active noise cancellation. The company's audio portfolio spans 107 products across categories including wireless earphones, wired earphones, headphones, microphones, and audio accessories, distributed through SoftBank stores and online channels.

## Technology Level

$$ \Large \text{0.7} $$

SoftBank SELECTION demonstrates solid contemporary technology implementation in their flagship GLIDiC TW-9100. The product features in-house design combining Knowles balanced armature drivers with dynamic drivers, creating a desirable hybrid configuration that other manufacturers would want to adopt. The implementation includes adaptive hybrid active noise cancellation, AI noise reduction for calls, and multipoint Bluetooth connectivity. However, when evaluated against current 2026 state-of-the-art standards, the technology shows gaps compared to leading competitors that now offer Bluetooth 6.0, advanced AI processing algorithms, superior codec support (aptX Lossless, LDAC), and latest ANC implementations. The GLIDiC product relies on older Bluetooth standards and limited codec support (AAC/SBC only) compared to competitors implementing cutting-edge connectivity and processing technologies. While the company doesn't possess proprietary patent technology (relying on Knowles components), their design expertise shows appropriate integration of audio technologies that were cutting-edge for 2025 standards. The hybrid driver approach represents a technically sophisticated solution for achieving extended frequency response, though competitive advantage duration is moderate as similar configurations are becoming more common in the premium true wireless segment.
