## Overview

Whizzer is a Chinese professional audio brand founded in 2015 and based in Shenzhen. It operates three sub-brands—Kylin, OperaFactory, and Easytips—and focuses on in-ear earphones along with cables, accessories, and Bluetooth products. The company states that products are independently designed and tuned by its J.IDEA studio.[1]

## Cost-Performance

$$ \Large \text{0.3} $$

This site evaluates based solely on functionality and measured performance values, without considering driver types or configurations. Representative products selected are HE01 and HE03D. Current market prices from the official store are 55.00 USD and 90.00 USD respectively.[2][3] The comparison target is Moondrop CHU II at 22.99 USD, selected as the lowest-priced option with published numeric specs and stable availability.[4]

For HE01, equivalence is based on matching user-facing functions (wired in-ear, 3.5mm plug, 0.78mm 2pin detachable cable) and manufacturer specs showing audible-band coverage, equal impedance at 18Ω, higher sensitivity at 119dB/Vrms, and numerically lower THD at 0.5% or below (1kHz, 94dB), noting differing test conditions.[2][4] CP = 22.99 USD ÷ 55.00 USD = 0.418.

For HE03D, the same comparator applies. User-facing functions match, and CHU II provides lower impedance and higher sensitivity. THD is numerically lower but measured at a different SPL, so the equivalence is provisional.[3][4] CP = 22.99 USD ÷ 90.00 USD = 0.255.

Weighting uses HE01 at 0.4 and HE03D at 0.6. Average CP is (0.418 × 0.4) + (0.255 × 0.6) = 0.320, rounded to 0.3. Comparison remains provisional due to reliance on manufacturer specs.
