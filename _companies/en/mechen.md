## Overview

MECHEN is a Chinese technology company operated by Shenzhen Yunmei Industrial Co., Ltd., specializing in budget-oriented digital audio equipment [3]. Founded as a cost-focused manufacturer, the company primarily distributes through Amazon and other online marketplaces rather than traditional retail channels [2]. MECHEN's product lineup centers on Digital Audio Players (DAPs) and USB DAC dongles positioned as affordable alternatives to established brands like FiiO, HiBy, and Shanling. Representative products include the M30 DAP (109 USD) featuring DSD support and metal construction [1], and the AP10 USB DAC dongle (35-40 USD) with balanced output capability [2]. The company maintains minimal corporate communication and focuses on warranty support rather than brand marketing [3], targeting entry-level audiophiles and budget-conscious consumers seeking high-resolution audio capabilities without premium pricing.
